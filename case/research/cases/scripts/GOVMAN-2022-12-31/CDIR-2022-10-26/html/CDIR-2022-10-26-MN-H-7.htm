    MICHELLE FISCHBACH, Republican, of Regal, MN; born in MN, November 
3, 1965; education: B.A., political science and economics, Saint Cloud 
State University, St. Cloud, MN, 1989; J.D., William Mitchell School of 
Law, St. Paul, MN, 2011; professional: member, Paynesville, MN City 
Council, 1995-96; Minnesota State Senator, 1996-2018, serving as 
Assistant Minority Leader, 2001-02, 2007-08; Deputy Minority Leader, 
2009-10; President, 2011-13, 2017-18; lieutenant governor, state of 
Minnesota, 2018-19; religion: Catholic; family: husband; two children; 
six grandchildren; committees: Agriculture; Judiciary; Rules; elected to 
the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
