    GERALD E. CONNOLLY, Democrat, of Fairfax, VA; born in Boston, MA, 
March 30, 1950; education: B.A., Maryknoll College; M.A., public 
administration, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 1979; professional: 
member, Fairfax County Board of Supervisors, 1995-2003, chairman, 2003-
09; religion: Roman Catholic; married: Cathy; children: Caitlin; 
committees: Foreign Affairs; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 111th 
Congress on November 4, 2008; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
