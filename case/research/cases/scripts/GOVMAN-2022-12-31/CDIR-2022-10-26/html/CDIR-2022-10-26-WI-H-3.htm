    RON KIND, Democrat, of La Crosse, WI; born in La Crosse, March 16, 
1963; education: B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 1985; M.A., 
London School of Economics, London, England, 1986; J.D., University of 
Minnesota Law School, Minneapolis, MN, 1990; professional: admitted to 
the Wisconsin Bar, 1990; State Prosecutor, La Crosse County District 
Attorney's Office; board of directors, La Crosse Boys and Girls Club; 
Coulee Council on Alcohol and Drug Abuse; Wisconsin Harvard Club; 
Wisconsin Bar Association; La Crosse County Bar Association; married: 
Tawni Zappa in 1994; two sons: Jonathan and Matthew; committees: Ways 
and Means; elected to the 105th Congress on November 5, 1996; reelected 
to each succeeding Congress.
