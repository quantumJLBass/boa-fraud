    RAND PAUL, Republican, of Bowling Green, KY; born in Pittsburgh, 
PA, January 7, 1963; education: undergraduate, Baylor University, Waco, 
Texas, 1981-84; M.D., Duke University School of Medicine, 1988; 
religion: Methodist; family: married to the former Kelley Ashby; three 
sons: William, Duncan, and Robert; committees: ranking member, Small 
Business and Entrepreneurship; Foreign Relations; Health, Education, 
Labor, and Pensions; Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs; elected 
to the U.S. Senate on November 2, 2010; reelected to the U.S. Senate on 
November 8, 2016.
