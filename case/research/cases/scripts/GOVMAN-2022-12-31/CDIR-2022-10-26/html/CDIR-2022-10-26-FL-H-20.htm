    SHEILA CHERFILUS-McCORMICK, Democrat, of Miramar, FL; born in 
Brooklyn, NY, January 25, 1979; education: B.A., Howard University, 
Washington, DC, 2001; J.D., St. Thomas University School of Law, Miami 
Gardens, FL, 2010; professional: health care executive; committees: 
Education and Labor; Transportation and Infrastructure; Veterans' 
Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress, by special election, on January 
11, 2022, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of U.S. Representative 
Alcee Hastings.
