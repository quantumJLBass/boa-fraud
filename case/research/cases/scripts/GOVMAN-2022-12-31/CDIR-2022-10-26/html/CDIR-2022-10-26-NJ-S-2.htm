    CORY A. BOOKER, Democrat, of Newark, NJ; born in Washington, DC, 
April 27, 1969; education: graduated, Northern Valley Regional High 
School at Old Tappan, 1987; B.A., political science, Stanford 
University, Stanford, CA, 1991; M.A., sociology, Stanford University, 
1992; Oxford University Rhodes Scholar, Oxford, England, 1994; J.D., 
Yale Law School, New Haven, CT, 1997; professional: staff attorney, 
Urban Justice Center, New York, NY, 1997; member, Newark City Council, 
1998-2002; partner, Booker, Rabinowitz, Trenk, Lubetkin, Tully, 
DiPasquale & Webster, P.C., 2002-06; Mayor, City of Newark, 2006-13; 
religion: Baptist; committees: Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry; 
Foreign Relations; Judiciary; Small Business and Entrepreneurship; 
elected to the U.S. Senate on October 16, 2013, by special election, to 
fill the vacancy caused by the death of Senator Frank R. Lautenberg; 
reelected to each succeeding Senate term.
