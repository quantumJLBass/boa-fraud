    BILL CASSIDY, Republican, of Baton Rouge, LA; born in Highland 
Park, IL, September 28, 1957; education: graduated, Tara High School; 
B.S., Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, 1979; M.D., Louisiana 
State University Medical School, New Orleans, LA, 1983; professional: 
medical doctor, Baton Rouge; associate professor of medicine with LSU 
Health Sciences Center; member of the Louisiana State Senate; married: 
Laura Layden Cassidy, M.D.; children: Will, Meg, and Kate; committees: 
Energy and Natural Resources; Finance; Health, Education, Labor, and 
Pensions; Veterans' Affairs; Joint Economic Committee; elected to the 
111th Congress; reelected to the 112th and 113th Congresses; won the 
runoff election to the U.S. Senate on December 6, 2014; reelected to the 
U.S. Senate on November 3, 2020.
