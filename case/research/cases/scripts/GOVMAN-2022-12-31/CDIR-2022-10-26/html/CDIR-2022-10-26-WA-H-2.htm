    RICK LARSEN, Democrat, of Everett, WA; born in Arlington, WA, June 
15, 1965; education: B.A., Pacific Lutheran University, Tacoma, WA; 
M.P.A., University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN; professional: economic 
development official at the Port of Everett; director of public affairs 
for a health provider association; public service: Snohomish County 
Council; religion: Methodist; married: Tiia Karlen; children: Robert and 
Per; committees: Armed Services; Transportation and Infrastructure; 
elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
