13, 1971; education: A.S., economics, Marion Military Institute, Marion, 
AL, 1991; B.A., history, Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, 1993; 
professional: military; officer, U.S. Army Reserve, 1989-99; staff, Ohio 
state senator Merle Grace Kearns, 1991-93; staff, U.S. Representative 
Robert Wil

liam Ney of Ohio, 1995-98; professional advocate; business executive; 
committees: Budget. Science, Space, and Technology; elected to the 117th

caused by the resignation of U.S. Representative Steve Stivers.
