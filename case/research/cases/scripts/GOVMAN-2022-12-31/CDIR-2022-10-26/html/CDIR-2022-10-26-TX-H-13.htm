    RONNY JACKSON, Republican, of Amarillo, TX; born in Levelland, TX, 
May 4, 1967; education: A.S., South Plains College, Levelland, TX; B.S., 
Texas Agricultural and Mechanical University, Galveston, TX, 1991; M.D., 
University of Texas Medical Branch, Galveston, TX, 1995; professional: 
United States Navy Reserve; United States Navy, 1995-2019, Rear Admiral; 
physician, White House Medical Unit, 2006-18; Physician to the 
President, 2013-18; Assistant to the President and Chief Medical 
Advisor, Executive Office of the President, Donald J. Trump, 2019; 
family: wife, Jane Jackson; three children; committees: Armed Services; 
Foreign Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
