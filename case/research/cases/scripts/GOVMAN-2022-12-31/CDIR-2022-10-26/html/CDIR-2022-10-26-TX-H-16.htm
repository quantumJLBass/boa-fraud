    VERONICA ESCOBAR, Democrat, of El Paso, TX; born in El Paso, 
September 15, 1969; education: B.A., University of Texas at El Paso, El 
Paso, TX, 1991; M.A., New York University, New York City, NY, 1993; 
professional: college professor; nonprofit executive; communications 
director; El Paso County Commissioner, 2006-10; El Paso County Judge, 
2011-17; committees: Armed Services; Ethics; Judiciary; Select Committee 
on the Climate Crisis; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 
2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
