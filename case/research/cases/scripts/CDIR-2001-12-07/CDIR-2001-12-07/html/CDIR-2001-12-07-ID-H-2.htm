    MICHAEL K. SIMPSON, Republican, of Blackfoot, ID; born in Burley, 
ID, on September 8, 1950; education: graduated, Blackfoot High School, 
1968; Utah State University, 1972; Washington University School of 
Dental Medicine, 1977; employment: dentist, private practice; Blackfoot, 
ID, City Council, 1981-85; Idaho State Legislature, 1985-98; Idaho 
Speaker of the House 1992-98; married: Kathy Simpson; committees: 
Agriculture; Resources; Transportation and Infrastructure; Veterans' 
Affairs; elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
