    JOHN F. TIERNEY, Democrat, of Salem, MA; born on September 18, 1951 
in Salem; graduated, Salem High School; B.A., political science, Salem 
State College, 1973; J.D., Suffolk University, 1976; attorney, admitted 
to the Massachusetts bar in 1976; sole practitioner, 1976-80; partner, 
Tierney, Kalis and Lucas, 1981-96; member: Salem Chamber of Commerce, 
1976-96 (president, 1995); trustee, Salem State College, 1992-97; 
married Patrice M. Tierney, 1997; elected to the 105th Congress; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
