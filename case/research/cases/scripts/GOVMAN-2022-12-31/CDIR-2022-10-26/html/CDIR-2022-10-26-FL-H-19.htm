    BYRON DONALDS, Republican, of Naples, FL; born in Brooklyn, NY, 
October 28, 1978; education: B.S., Florida State University, 
Tallahassee, FL, 2002; professional: financial advisor; member, Florida 
State House of Representatives, 2016-21; religion: Christian; married: 
Erika Donalds; children: Damon, Darin, and Mason; committees: Budget; 
Oversight and Reform; Small Business; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
