    MARY GAY SCANLON, Democrat, of Swarthmore, PA; born in Watertown, 
NY, August 30, 1959; education: B.A., Colgate University, Hamilton, NY, 
1980; J.D., University of Pennsylvania Law School, Philadelphia, PA, 
1984; professional: attorney, Education Law Center, Philadelphia, PA; 
pro bono counsel, Ballard Spahr, LLP, Philadelphia, PA; administration 
chair of Communications Standard Commission; appointed vice chair of 
Wallingford-Swarthmore Tax Commission, 2006-07; elected member, 
Wallingford-Swarthmore School Board, 2007-15, president, 2009-11; 
family: husband, Mark Stewart; children: Casey, Daniel, and Matthew; 
committees: House Administration; Judiciary; Rules; elected 
simultaneously, by special election, on November 6, 2018 to represent 
the Seventh District in the 115th and the Fifth District in the 116th 
Congresses to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of U.S. 
Representative Patrick Meehan; reelected to the Fifth District for the 
117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
