    MARIA CANTWELL, Democrat, of Edmonds, WA; born on October 13, 1958, 
in Indianapolis, IN; education: B.A., Miami University, Miami, OH, 1980; 
professional: businesswoman; RealNetworks, Inc.; organizations: South 
Snohomish County Chamber of Commerce; Alderwood Rotary; Mountlake 
Terrace Friends of the Library; public service: Washington State House 
of Representatives, 1987-1992; U.S. House of Representatives, 1992-1994; 
religion; Roman Catholic; elected to the U.S. Senate on November 7, 
2000.
