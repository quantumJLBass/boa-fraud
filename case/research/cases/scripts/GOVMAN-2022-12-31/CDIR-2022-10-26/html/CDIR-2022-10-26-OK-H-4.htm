    TOM COLE, Republican, of Moore, OK; born in Shreveport, LA, April 
28, 1949; education: B.A., Grinnell College, 1971; M.A., Yale 
University, 1974; Ph.D., University of Oklahoma, 1984; Watson Fellow, 
1971-72; Fulbright Fellow, 1977-78; professional: former college 
professor of history and politics; president, Cole Hargrave Snodgrass & 
Associates (political consulting firm); public service: Oklahoma State 
Senate, 1988-91; Oklahoma Secretary of State, 1995-99; has served as 
chairman, and executive director, of the Oklahoma Republican Party; 
former chairman of the National Republican Congressional Committee; and 
chief of staff of the Republican National Committee; family: married to 
Ellen; one child: Mason; religion: United Methodist; committees: ranking 
member, Rules; Appropriations; elected to the 108th Congress on November 
5, 2002; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
