    RAUL M. GRIJALVA, Democrat, of Tucson, AZ; born in Tucson, February 
19, 1948; education: Sunnyside High School, Tucson, AZ, 1967; B.A., 
sociology, University of Arizona, Tucson, AZ, 1987; professional: former 
Assistant Dean for Hispanic Student Affairs, University of Arizona; 
former Director of the El Pueblo Neighborhood Center; public service: 
Tucson Unified School District Governing Board, 1974-86; Pima County 
Board of Supervisors, 1989-2002; family: married to Ramona; three 
daughters; committees: chair, Natural Resources; Education and Labor; 
elected to the 108th Congress on November 5, 2002; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
