    DEAN PHILLIPS, Democrat, of Deephaven, MN; born in St. Paul, MN, 
January 20, 1969; education: B.A., urban studies, Brown University, 
Providence, RI, 1991; M.B.A., Carlson School of Management, University 
of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, 2000; professional: President / CEO, 
1993-2012, former chair of the board, 2014-17, Phillips Distilling 
Company; former chair, Talenti Gelato; co-founder, Penny's Coffee, 2016; 
committees: Ethics; Foreign Affairs; Small Business; elected to the 
116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
