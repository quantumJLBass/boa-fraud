    DAN BISHOP, Republican, of Charlotte, NC; born in Charlotte, July 
1, 1964; education: B.S., business administration, 1986, J.D., 1990, 
University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, NC; professional: attorney, 
Robinson, Bradshaw, and Hinson, Charlotte, NC, 1990-96; attorney, Erwin, 
Bishop, Capitano, and Moss, Charlotte, NC, 1996-present; Commissioner, 
District 5, Mecklenburg County Board of Commissioners, Charlotte, NC, 
2004-08; Representative, 104th District, North Carolina House of 
Representatives, Raleigh, NC, 2015-16; Senator, 39th District, North 
Carolina Senate, Raleigh, NC, 2017-19; religion: Methodist; family: 
wife, Jo; one child: Jack; committees: Homeland Security; Judiciary; 
elected to the 116th Congress, by special election, on September 10, 
2019, to fill a vacancy in the district; reelected to the 117th Congress 
on November 3, 2020.
