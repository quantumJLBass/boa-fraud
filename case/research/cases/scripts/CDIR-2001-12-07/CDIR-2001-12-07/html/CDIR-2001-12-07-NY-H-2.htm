    STEVE ISRAEL, Democrat, of Dix Hills, NY; born on May 30, 1958, in 
Brooklyn, NY; education: B.A., George Washington University, 1982; 
profession: public relations and marketing executive; public service: 
Legislative Assistant for Rep. Richard Ottinger (D-NY), 1980-83; Suffolk 
County Executive for Intergovernmental Relations, 1988-91; elected to 
the Huntington Town Board, 1993; reelected two times; organizations: 
Institute on the Holocaust; Touro Law Center; Nature Conservancy; 
Audubon Society; awards: Child Care Council of Suffolk Leadership Award; 
Anti-Defamation League and Sons of Italy Purple Aster Award; elected to 
the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
