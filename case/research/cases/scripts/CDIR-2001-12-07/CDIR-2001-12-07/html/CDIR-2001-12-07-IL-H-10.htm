    MARK STEVEN KIRK, Republican, of Kenilworth, IL; born on September 
15, 1959, in Champaign, IL; education: New Trier East High School, 
Winnetka, IL, 1977; B.A., Cornell University, 1981; J.D., Georgetown 
University, 1992; profession: Attorney; military service: Lt. Commander, 
U.S. Navy Reserve; employment: Administrative Assistant to Rep. John 
Porter (R-IL), 1984-90; World Bank, served as an International Finance 
Corp. officer; Dept. of State, served as Special Assistant to the 
Assistant Secretary for Inter-American Affairs; Baker & McKenzie (law 
firm); House Committee on International Relations, served as Counsel; 
married: Kimberly Vertolli; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 
2000.
