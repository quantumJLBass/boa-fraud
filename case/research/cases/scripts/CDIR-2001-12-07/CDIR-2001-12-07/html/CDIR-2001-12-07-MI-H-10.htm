    DAVID E. BONIOR, Democrat, of Mount Clemens, MI; born in Detroit, 
MI, June 6, 1945; graduated, Notre Dame High School, 1963; B.A., 
University of Iowa, 1967; M.A., history, Chapman College (CA), 1972; 
served in the U.S. Air Force, 1968-72; member, Michigan House of 
Representatives, 1973-77; author, ``The Vietnam Veteran: A History of 
Neglect''; married to Judy Bonior; three children: Julie, Andy, and 
Stephen; elected to the 95th Congress, November 2, 1976; reelected to 
each succeeding Congress.
