    MAYRA FLORES, Republican, of McAllen, TX, born in Burgos, 
Tamaulipas, Mexico, January 1, 1986; education: B.A.S., South Texas 
College, McAllen, TX, 2019; professional: respiratory therapist; 
committees: Agriculture; Homeland Security; elected to the 117th 
Congress, by special election, on June 14, 2022, to fill the vacancy 
caused by the resignation of United States Representative Filemon Vela.
