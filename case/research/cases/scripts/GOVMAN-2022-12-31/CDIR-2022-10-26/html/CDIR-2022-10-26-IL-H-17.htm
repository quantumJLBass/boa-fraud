    CHERI BUSTOS, Democrat, of Moline, IL; born in Springfield, IL, 
October 17, 1961; education: B.A., University of Maryland, College Park, 
MD, 1983; M.A., University of Illinois Springfield, Springfield, IL, 
1985; religion: Roman Catholic; married: Gerry; children: Tony, Nick, 
and Joseph; committees: Agriculture; Appropriations; elected to the 
113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
