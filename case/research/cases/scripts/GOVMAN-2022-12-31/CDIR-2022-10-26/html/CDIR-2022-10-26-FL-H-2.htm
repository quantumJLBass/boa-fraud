    NEAL P. DUNN, Republican, of Panama City, FL; born in New Haven, 
CT, February 16, 1953; education: B.A., Washington and Lee University, 
Lexington, VA; M.D., George Washington University Medical School, 
Washington, DC; residency, Walter Reed Army Medical Center; 
professional: founding president, Advanced Urology Institute, Panama 
City, FL; founding chair, Summit Bank, Panama City, FL; member, board of 
governors, Florida Medical Association; military: Major, U.S. Army 
(Ret.); religion: Roman Catholic; married: Leah Ott Dunn; three 
children; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected to the 115th Congress 
on November 8, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
