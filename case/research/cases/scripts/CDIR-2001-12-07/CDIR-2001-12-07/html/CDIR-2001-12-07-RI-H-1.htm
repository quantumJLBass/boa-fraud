    PATRICK JOSEPH KENNEDY, Democrat, of Providence, RI; born on July 
14, 1967, in Brighton, MA; graduated, Phillips Academy, Andover, MA; 
B.A., Providence College, Providence, RI, 1991; Rhode Island State 
Legislature, 1988-94; member: Rhode Island Special Olympics (board of 
directors), Rhode Island March of Dimes, Rhode Island Lung Association, 
Rhode Island Mental Health Association, Rhode Island Chapter of National 
Committee for 
the Prevention of Child Abuse; elected to the 104th Congress; reelected 
to each succeeding 
Congress.
