    CAROL D. MILLER, Republican, of Huntington, WV; born in Columbus, 
OH, November 4, 1950; daughter of U.S. Representative Samuel L. Devine; 
education: B.S., history and political science, Columbia College, 
Columbia, SC, 1972; professional: member, West Virginia House of 
Delegates, 2007-19, becoming the first female Majority Whip; owner / 
operator, Swann Ridge Bison Farm; real estate manager; religion: 
Baptist; family: married to Matt; two children: Chris and Sam; six 
grandchildren; committees: Ways and Means; Select Committee on the 
Climate Crisis; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
