    SUE W. KELLY, Republican, of Katonah, NY; born in Lima, OH, 
September 26, 1936; graduated, Lima Central High School; B.A., Denison 
University, Granville, OH, 1958; M.A., Sarah Lawrence College, 
Bronxville, NY, 1985; educator, small business owner, patient advocate, 
rape crisis counselor, community leader; member: League of Women Voters, 
American Association of University Women, PTA, Bedford Recreation 
Committee; member, Bedford 

[[Page 185]]

Presbyterian Church; married Edward W. Kelly, 
1960; four children: Eric, Sean, Charity, and Tim; committees: 
Transportation and Infrastructure; Small Business; and Financial 
Services; elected to the 104th Congress; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
