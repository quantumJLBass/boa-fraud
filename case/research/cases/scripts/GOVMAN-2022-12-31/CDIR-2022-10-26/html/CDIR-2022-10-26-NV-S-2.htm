    JACKY ROSEN, Democrat, of Henderson, NV; born in Chicago, IL, 
August 2, 1957; education: B.A., University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, 
Minneapolis, MN, 1979; professional: computer programmer, software 
developer and designer for Summa Corporation; Citibank; Southwest Gas 
Company; awards: President of Congregation Ner Tamid; religion: Jewish; 
married: Larry Rosen; children: one; caucuses: Artificial Intelligence 
Caucus; Comprehensive Care Caucus; Congressional Asian Pacific American 
Caucus; Congressional Coalition on Adoption; Congressional TRIO Caucus; 
Cybersecurity Caucus; Cystic Fibrosis Caucus; Senate Cancer Coalition; 
Senate Caucus on International Narcotics Control; Senate Recycling 
Caucus; committees: Armed Services; Commerce, Science, and 
Transportation; Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions; Homeland 
Security and Governmental Affairs; Small Business and Entrepreneurship; 
Special Committee on Aging; elected to the 115th Congress on November 8, 
2016; elected to the U.S. Senate on November 6, 2018.
