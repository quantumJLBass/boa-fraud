    JOSEPH CROWLEY, Democrat, of Elmhurst, Queens, NY; born in New York, 
NY, on March 16, 1962; graduated: Power Memorial High School, 1981; 
B.A., Queens College, 1985; elected to the New York State Assembly, 
1986-98; Assembly Committees: Racing and Wagering; Banking, Consumer 
Affairs, and Protection; Election Law; Labor and Housing; religion: 
Roman Catholic; married to Kasey Nilson; committees: Financial Services; 
International Relations; elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 
107th Congress.
