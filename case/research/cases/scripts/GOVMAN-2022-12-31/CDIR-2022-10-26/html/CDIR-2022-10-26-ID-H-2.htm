    MICHAEL K. SIMPSON, Republican, of Blackfoot, ID; born in Burley, 
ID, September 8, 1950; education: graduated, Blackfoot High School, 
1968; Utah State University, 1972; Washington University School of 
Dental Medicine, 1977; professional: dentist, private practice; 
Blackfoot, ID, City Council, 1981-85; Idaho State Legislature, 1985-98; 
Idaho Speaker of the House, 1992-98; married: Kathy Simpson; committees: 
Appropriations; elected to the 106th Congress on November 3, 1998; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
