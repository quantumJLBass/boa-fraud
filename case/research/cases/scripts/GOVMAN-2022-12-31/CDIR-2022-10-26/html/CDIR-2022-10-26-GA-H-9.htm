    ANDREW S. CLYDE, Republican, of Athens, GA; born in Ontario, 
Canada, November 22, 1963; education: B.B.A., Bethel College, Mishawaka, 
IN, 1985; M.B.A., University of Georgia, Athens, GA, 1999; professional: 
U.S. Navy, 1985-96 (commissioned from the University of Notre Dame ROTC, 
Notre Dame, IN, 1985); U.S. Navy Reserve, 1996-2013; business owner; 
religion: Christian; family: wife, Jennifer; committees: Homeland 
Security; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
