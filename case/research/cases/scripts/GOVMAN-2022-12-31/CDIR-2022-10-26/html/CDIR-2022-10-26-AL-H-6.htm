    GARY PALMER, Republican, of Hoover, AL; born in Hackleburg, AL, May 
14, 1954; education: B.S., University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, 1977; 
professional: president, Alabama Policy Institute; founding member, 
State Policy Network; member, Briarwood Presbyterian Church; spouse: 
Ann; children: Claire, Kathleen, and Rob; committees: Energy and 
Commerce; Select Committee on the Climate Crisis; elected to the 114th 
Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
