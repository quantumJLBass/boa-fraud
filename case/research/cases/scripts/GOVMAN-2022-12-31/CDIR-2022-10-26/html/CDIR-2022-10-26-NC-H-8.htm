    RICHARD HUDSON, Republican, of Concord, NC; born in Franklin, VA, 
November 4, 1971; education: B.A. in history and political science, 
University of North Carolina at Charlotte, 1996; professional: served as 
District Director for 8th District Congressman Robin Hayes; served as 
Chief of Staff for Congresswoman Virginia Foxx, Congressman John Carter, 
and Congressman Mike Conaway; religion: Christian; married: Renee; 
caucuses: chair, Agriculture Policy Group; co-chair, Agriculture and 
Rural America Task Force; co-chair, Atlantic Offshore Energy Caucus; co-
chair, Carbonated and Non-alcoholic (CAN) Caucus; co-chair, 
Congressional Biodefense Caucus; co-chair, Congressional NextGen 9-1-1 
Caucus; co-chair, Congressional Tire Caucus; co-chair, Direct Selling 
Caucus; co-chair, EMS Caucus; co-chair, Global Road Safety Caucus; co-
chair, House Special Operations Forces Caucus; co-chair, Pediatric 
Trauma Caucus; vice chair, Congressional Sportsmen's Caucus; 
Congressional Army Aviation Caucus;

[[Page 201]]

Congressional Chicken Caucus; Congressional General Aviation Caucus; 
Congressional Peanut Caucus; Congressional Prayer Caucus; Congressional 
Textile Caucus; House National Guard and Reserve Components Caucus; 
House Republican Israel Caucus; Republican Policy Committee; Republican 
Study Committee; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected to the 113th 
Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
