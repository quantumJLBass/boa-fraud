    TOM McCLINTOCK, Republican, of Granite Bay, CA; born in 
Bronxville, NY, July 10, 1956; education: B.A.,  cum laude, political 
science, UCLA, Los Angeles, CA, 1978; professional: member, California 
State Assembly, 1982-92 and 1996-2000; member, California State Senate, 
2000-08; director, Center for the California Taxpayer, National Tax 
Limitation Foundation, 1992-94; director, Economic and Regulatory 
Affairs, Claremont Institute, 1994-96; married: Lori; two children; 
committees: Budget; Judiciary; Natural Resources; elected to the 111th 
Congress on November 4, 2008; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
