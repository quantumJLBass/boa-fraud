    BRAD FINSTAD, Republican, of New Ulm, MN; born in New Ulm, May 30, 
1976; education: B.S., University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, 
professional: staff, United States Representative Mark Kennedy of 
Minnesota, 2001-02; farmer; agriculture consultant and executive; member 
of the Minnesota state house of representatives, 2003-08; Minnesota 
state director, United States Department of Agriculture Rural 
Development, 2017-21; committees: Agriculture; Education and Labor; 
elected to the 117th Congress, by special election, on August 9, 2022, 
to fill the vacancy caused by the death of U.S. Representative Jim 
Hagedorn.
