    JODY B. HICE, Republican, of Greensboro, GA; born in Atlanta, GA, 
April 22, 1960; education: graduated, Tucker High School, Tucker, GA, 
1978; B.A., ministry, Asbury University, Wilmore, KY, 1982; M.Div., 
Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary, Fort Worth, TX, 1986; D.Min., 
Luther Rice College and Seminary, Lithonia, GA, 1988; professional: 
pastor; religion: Southern Baptist; married: Dee Dee Crocker Hice; 
children: Anna and Sara; grandchildren: Peter, Margaret, Alyssa, and 
Felix; committees: Natural Resources; Oversight and Reform; elected to 
the 114th Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
