    BILL HUIZENGA, Republican, of Zeeland, MI; born in Zeeland, January 
31, 1969; education: graduated, Holland Christian High School; B.A., 
Calvin College, Grand Rapids, MI, 1987; professional: co-owner, Huizenga 
Gravel Company, Jenison, MI; formerly licensed realtor and developer; 
married: the former Natalie Tiesma; children: Garrett, Adrian, 
Alexandra, William, and Sieger; committees: Financial Services; elected 
to the 112th Congress on November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
