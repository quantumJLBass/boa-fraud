    CLIFF BENTZ, Republican, of Ontario, OR; born in Salem, OR, January 
12, 1952; education: B.S., history, Eastern Oregon State College, La 
Grande, OR, 1974; J.D., Lewis and Clark Northwestern College of Law, 
Portland, OR, 1977; professional: attorney; farmer and owner / operator 
of commercial real estate business; commissioner, Oregon Water Resources 
Commission, 1988-96; member, Oregon State House of Representatives, 
2008-18; Oregon State Senator, 2018-20; religion: Roman Catholic; 
married: wife, Lindsay; two children: Allison and Scott; committees: 
Judiciary; Natural Resources; elected to the 117th Congress on November 
3, 2020.
