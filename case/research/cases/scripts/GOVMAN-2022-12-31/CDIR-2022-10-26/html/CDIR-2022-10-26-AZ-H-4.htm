    PAUL GOSAR, Republican, of Prescott, AZ; born in Rock Springs, WY, 
November 27, 1958; education: graduated, Pinedale High School, Pinedale, 
WY; B.S., Creighton University,

[[Page 13]]

Omaha, NE, 1981; D.D.S., Creighton University, Omaha, 1985; religion: 
Catholic; family: wife, Maude; children: Elle, Gaston, and Isabelle; 
caucuses: Coal Caucus; GOP Doctor's Caucus; House Freedom Caucus; 
Western Caucus; elected to the 112th Congress on November 2, 2010; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
