    CARLOS A. GIMENEZ, Republican, of Miami, FL; born in Cuba, January 
17, 1954; education: B.P.A., Barry University, Miami, FL, 1999; 
professional: firefighter; chief, Miami, FL fire-rescue department, 
1991-2000; city manager, Miami, FL, 2000-03; commissioner, Miami-Dade 
County, FL, 2004-11; Mayor, Miami-Dade County, FL, 2011-20; family: 
married; three children; six grandchildren; caucuses: Colombia Caucus; 
Everglades Caucus; Former Local Elected Officials Caucus; Latino Jewish 
Congressional Caucus; Republican Study Committee; Venezuela Democracy 
Caucus; committees: Homeland Security; Science, Space, and Technology; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
