    TOM COTTON, Republican, of Dardanelle, AR; born in Dardanelle, AR, 
May 13, 1977; education: graduated Dardanelle High School; B.A.,  magna 
cum laude, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 1999; J.D., Harvard 
University, 2002; professional: attorney; management consultant; 
military service: U.S. Army Infantry Officer, 2005-09; awards: Ranger 
Tab Recipient; Army Commendation Medal; Combat Infantryman Badge; Iraq 
Campaign Medal; Bronze Star Medal; committees: Armed Services; 
Judiciary; Joint Economic Committee; Select Committee on Intelligence; 
elected to the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; elected to the U.S. 
Senate on November 4, 2014; reelected to the U.S. Senate on November 3, 
2020.
