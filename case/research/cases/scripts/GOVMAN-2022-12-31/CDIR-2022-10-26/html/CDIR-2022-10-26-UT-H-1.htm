    BLAKE D. MOORE, Republican, of Salt Lake City, UT; born in Ogden, 
UT, June 22, 1980; education: B.A., University of Utah, Salt Lake City, 
UT, 2005; M.P.P.A., Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 2018; 
professional: Foreign Service Officer, U.S. Department of State; 
Consultant, Cicero Group; religion: Member of the Church of Jesus Christ 
of Latter-day Saints; family: married; three sons; committees: Armed 
Services; Budget; Natural Resources; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
