    PAT FALLON, Republican, of Sherman, TX; born in Pittsfield, MA, 
December 19, 1967; education: B.A., Government and International 
Relations, University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, IN, 1990; professional: 
entrepreneur; business executive; member: Football National Championship 
Football team, University of Notre Dame, 1988; Frisco city council, 
2009-12 mayor pro tem, Frisco, TX, 2011-12; Texas House of 
Representatives, 2013-19; Texas State Senate, 2019-21; delegate, 
Republican National Convention, 2016; military: United States Air Force; 
religion: Catholic; family: wife, Susan; children: Thomas and Mac; 
committees: Armed Services; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 117th 
Congress on November 3, 2020.
