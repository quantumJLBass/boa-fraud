    RODNEY DAVIS, Republican, of Taylorville, IL; born in Des Moines, 
IA, January 5, 1970; education: graduated from Taylorville High School, 
1988; B.A., Millikin University, IL, 1992;

[[Page 96]]

professional: congressional aide, 1999-2012; has served on numerous 
local civic and community organizations and groups; religion: Catholic; 
married: Shannon R. Davis; children: Toryn, Clark, and Griffin; 
committees: ranking member, House Administration; Agriculture; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 113th Congress on 
November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
