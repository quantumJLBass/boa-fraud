    STEVE WOMACK, Republican, of Rogers, AR; born in Russellville, AR, 
February 18, 1957; education: Russellville High School, Russellville, 
AR; B.A., Arkansas Tech University, Russellville, AR, 1979; 
professional: radio station manager; financial consultant; Mayor of 
Rogers, AR; military: retired colonel, National Guard; awards: Legion of 
Merit; Meritorious Service Medal; Army Commendation Medal; Army 
Achievement Medal; Global War on Terror Expeditionary and Service 
Medals; religion: Southern Baptist; family: married the former Terri 
Williams of DeWitt, AR; three sons; caucuses: Congressional Chicken 
Caucus; committees: Appropriations; elected to the 112th Congress on 
November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
