    DUNCAN HUNTER, Republican, of Alpine, CA; born in Riverside, CA, May 
31, 1948; education: graduated, Rubidoux High School, 1966; J.D., 
Western State University, 1976; first lieutenant, U.S. Army Airborne, 
1969-71; profession: trial lawyer; admitted to the California bar, 

[[Page 44]]

1976; 
commenced practice in San Diego; member: Baptist Church, Navy League; 
married: the former Lynne Layh, 1973; children: Duncan Duane and Robert 
Samuel; elected to the 97th Congress, November 4, 1980; reelected to 
each succeeding Congress.
