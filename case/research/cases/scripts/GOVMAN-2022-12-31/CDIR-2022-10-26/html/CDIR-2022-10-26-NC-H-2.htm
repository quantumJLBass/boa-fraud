    DEBORAH K. ROSS, Democrat, of Raleigh, NC; born in Philadelphia, 
PA, June 20, 1963; education: A.B., Brown University, Providence, RI, 
1985; J.D., University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, NC, 1990; 
professional: lawyer, private practice; state director, American Civil 
Liberties Union, 1994-2002; member, North Carolina State House of 
Representatives; 2003-13; religion: Unitarian; committees: Judiciary; 
Rules; Science, Space, and Technology; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
