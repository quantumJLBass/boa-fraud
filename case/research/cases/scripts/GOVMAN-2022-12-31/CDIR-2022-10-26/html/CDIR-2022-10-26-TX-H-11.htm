    AUGUST PFLUGER, Republican, of San Angelo, TX; born in Harris 
County, TX, December 28, 1977; education: B.S., United States Air Force 
Academy, Colorado Springs, CO, 2000; M.S.A., Embry-Riddle Aeronautical 
University, Daytona Beach, FL, 2007; M.S., Air University, Montgomery, 
AL, 2012; M.A., Georgetown University, Washington, DC, 2019; 
professional: U.S. Air Force; U.S. Air Force Reserve; staff, National 
Security Council, 2019; religion: Protestant; married: Camille; 
children: Vivian, Caroline, and Juliana; committees: Foreign Affairs; 
Homeland Security; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
