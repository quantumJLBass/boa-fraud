    SHERROD BROWN, Democrat, of Cleveland, OH; born in Mansfield, OH, 
November 9, 1952; education: B.A., Yale University, New Haven, CT, 1974; 
M.A., education, Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, 1979; M.A., public 
administration, Ohio State University, Columbus, 1981; professional: 
Ohio House of Representatives, 1975-82; Ohio Secretary of State, 1983-
91; U.S. House of Representatives, 1992-2006; member: Eagle Scouts of 
America; married: Connie Schultz; children: Emily, Elizabeth, Andrew, 
and Caitlin; committees: chair, Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs; 
Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry; Finance; Veterans' Affairs; 
elected to the 103rd Congress on November 3, 1992; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress; elected to the U.S. Senate on November 7, 2006; 
reelected to each succeeding Senate term.
