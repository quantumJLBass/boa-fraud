    DANIEL T. KILDEE, Democrat, of Flint Township, MI; born in Flint, 
August 11, 1958; education: graduated, Northern High School, 1976; B.S., 
administration, Central Michigan University, 2011; married: Jennifer, 
1988; children: Ryan, Kenneth, and Katy; two grandchildren, Caitlin and 
Colin; Chief Deputy Whip; Democratic Steering and Policy Committee; 
committees: Budget; Science, Space, and Technology; Ways and Means; 
elected to the 113th Congress, November 6, 2012; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
