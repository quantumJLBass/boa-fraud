    JERROLD NADLER, Democrat, of New York, NY; born in Brooklyn, NY, 
June 13, 1947; education: Stuyvesant High School, New York, NY, 1965; 
B.A., Columbia University, New York, NY, 1969; J.D., Fordham University, 
Bronx, NY, 1978; professional: New York State Assembly, 1977-92; member: 
ACLU; NARAL Pro-Choice America; AIPAC; National Organization for Women; 
married: 1976; one child; Assistant Whip; committees: chair, Judiciary; 
elected simultaneously to the 102nd Congress, by special election to 
fill the vacancy caused by the death of U.S. Representative Ted Weiss, 
and the 103rd Congress on November 3, 1992; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
