    MIKE ROGERS, Republican, of Saks, AL; born in Hammond, IN, July 16, 
1958; education: B.A., Jacksonville State University, 1981; M.P.A., 
Jacksonville State University, Jacksonville, AL, 1984; J.D., Birmingham 
School of Law, 1991; professional: attorney; awards: Anniston Star 
Citizen of the Year, 1998; public service: Calhoun County Commissioner, 
1987-91; Alabama House of Representatives, 1994-2002; family: married to 
Beth; children: Emily, Evan, and Elliot; committees: Armed Services; 
elected to the 108th Congress on November 5, 2002; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
