    NANCY MACE, Republican, of Charleston, SC; born in Fayetteville, 
NC, December 4, 1977; education: B.A., business administration, The 
Citadel, The Military College of South Carolina, Charleston, SC, 1999; 
M.A., journalism and mass communications, University of Georgia, Athens, 
GA, 2004; professional: founder and president, The Mace Group, multi-
service public relations / marketing / web services business consulting 
group, Charleston, SC; author, In The Company of Men: a Woman at the 
Citadel, Simon and Schuster, 2001; member, South Carolina State House of 
Representatives, 2018-20; family: two children; caucuses: Air Cargo 
Caucus; Allergy and Asthma Caucus; Boating Caucus; Child Cancer Caucus; 
Congressional Sportsmen's Caucus; General Aviation Caucus; Global 
Investment and American Jobs Caucus; Live! Caucus; Tire Caucus; Travel 
and Tourism Caucus; committees: Oversight and Reform; Transportation and 
Infrastructure; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
