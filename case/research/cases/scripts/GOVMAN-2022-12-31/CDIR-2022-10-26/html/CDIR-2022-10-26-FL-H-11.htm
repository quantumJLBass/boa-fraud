    DANIEL WEBSTER, Republican, of Clermont, FL; born in Charleston, 
WV, April 27, 1949; education: graduated from Evans High School, 
Orlando, FL; B.S., Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, 1971; 
professional: president, Webster Air Conditioning & Heating, Inc., 
Orlando, FL; Florida House of Representatives, 1980-98; Speaker, Florida 
House of Representatives, 1996-98; Florida Senate, 1998-2008; Senate 
Majority Leader, Florida Senate, 2006-08; married: Sandy Jordan; father 
of six children and grandfather of 20; committees: Natural Resources; 
Science, Space, and Technology; Transportation and Infrastructure; 
elected to represent the 8th District in the 112th Congress on November 
2, 2010; elected to represent the 10th District in the 113th Congress on 
November 6, 2012, and reelected to the 114th Congress on November 4, 
2014; elected to represent the 11th District in the 115th Congress on 
November 8, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
