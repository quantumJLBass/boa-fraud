    DEBBIE LESKO, Republican, of Peoria, AZ; born November 14, 1958; 
education: B.B.A., University of Wisconsin, Madison, WI; professional / 
awards: business owner; Arizona State Representative, Legislative 
District 21, 2008-12 (Majority Whip, 2011-12); Arizona State Senator, 
Legislative District 21, 2015-18 (President Pro Tempore, 2017-18; chair, 
Appropriations Committee); Republican Chairman, Legislative District 9; 
voter registration chair, Maricopa County Republican Party; Senator of 
the Year Award, Arizona Chamber of Commerce; religion: Christian; 
married; three children; caucuses: Air Force Caucus; Army Caucus; 
Bipartisan Working Group to End Domestic Violence; Border Security 
Caucus; Candy Caucus; Colorado River Caucus; Congressional Caucus for 
Women's Issues; Congressional Critical Materials Caucus; Congressional 
Military Family Caucus; Congressional Valley Fever Task Force; Creative 
Rights
