    JASON CROW, Democrat, of Aurora, CO; born in Madison, WI, March 15, 
1979; education: B.A., University of Wisconsin, Madison, WI, 2002; J.D., 
University of Denver Sturm College of Law, 2009; military: United States 
Army, 2002-06; Bronze Star; professional: attorney, Holland & Hart LLP, 
2009-19; committees: Armed Services; Small Business; Permanent Select 
Committee on Intelligence; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 
2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
