    WARREN DAVIDSON, Republican, of Troy, OH; born in Sidney, OH, March 
1, 1970; education: B.S., American history, United States Military 
Academy, West Point, NY, 1995; M.B.A., University of Notre Dame, South 
Bend, IN, 2005; professional: managing director, West Troy, 2000-15; 
president, Global Source Manufacturing, 2002-15; founder, Factory Techs, 
2008-15; managing director, RK Metals, 2014-15; managing director, 
Integral Manufacturing, 2015-16; religion: Christian; family: married, 
two children; committees: Financial Services; elected to the 114th 
Congress, by special election, on June 7, 2016, to fill the vacancy 
caused by the resignation of U.S. Representative John Andrew Boehner; 
sworn in on June 9, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
