    THOMAS R. SUOZZI, Democrat, of Glen Cove, NY; born in Glen Cove, 
August 31, 1962; education: B.S., accounting, Boston College 1984; J.D., 
Fordham Law School, 1989; professional: certified public accountant; 
attorney; Glen Cove Mayor, 1994-2002; Nassau County executive, 2002-10; 
religion: Catholic; family: wife: Helene; three children; caucuses: co-
chair, Long Island Sound Caucus; co-chair, Quiet Skies Caucus; vice 
chair, Problem Solvers Caucus;

[[Page 183]]

committees: Ways and Means; elected to the 115th Congress on November 8, 
2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
