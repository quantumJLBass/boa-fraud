                                 SENATORS

    CHUCK GRASSLEY, Republican, of New Hartford, IA; born in New 
Hartford, September 17, 1933; education: graduated, New Hartford 
Community High School, 1951; B.A., University of Northern Iowa, Cedar 
Falls, IA, 1955; M.A., University of Northern Iowa, 1956; doctoral 
studies, University of Iowa, Iowa City, IA, 1957-58; professional: 
farmer; member: Iowa State Legislature, 1959-74; Farm Bureau; Butler 
County and State of Iowa Historical Societies; Masons; Baptist Church; 
and International Association of Machinists, 1962-71; married: the 
former Barbara Ann Speicher, 1954; children: Lee, Wendy, Robin, Michele, 
and Jay; caucuses: founder and co-chair, Senate Whistleblower Protection 
Caucus; co-founder and co-chair, Senate Caucus on Foster Youth; co-
founder, Senate Cystic Fibrosis; vice chair, Senate Caucus on 
International Narcotics Control; Rural Broadband Caucus; committees: 
ranking member, Judiciary; Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry; Budget; 
Finance; Joint Committee on Taxation; elected to the 94th Congress on 
November 5, 1974; reelected to the 95th and 96th Congresses; elected to 
the U.S. Senate on November 4, 1980; reelected to each succeeding Senate 
term.
