    SETH MOULTON, Democrat, of Salem, MA; born in Salem, October 24, 
1978; education: Phillips Academy Andover, 1997; A.B., Harvard College, 
Cambridge, MA, 2001; M.B.A., Harvard Business School, Boston, MA, 2011; 
M.P.A., Harvard Kennedy School of Government, Cambridge, MA, 2011; 
professional: U.S. Marine Corps, 2002-08; business manager; committees: 
Armed Services; Budget; Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to 
the 114th Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
