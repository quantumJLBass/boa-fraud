    JOHN J. LaFALCE, Democrat, of Tonawanda, NY; born in Buffalo, 
October 6, 1939; education: graduated from Canisius High School; 
Canisius College, B.S. 1961; Villanova Law School, J.D. 1964; military 
service: captain, U.S. Army, 1965-67; law clerk, Office of General 
Counsel, Department of the Navy, 1963; former lecturer on law, George 
Washington University, 1965-66; practiced law in Buffalo; member, New 
York State Senate, 1971-72; member, New York State Assembly, 1973-74; 
ranking minority member, Committee on Financial Services; married: 
Patricia Fisher, 1979; elected to the 94th Congress, November 5, 1974; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress;
