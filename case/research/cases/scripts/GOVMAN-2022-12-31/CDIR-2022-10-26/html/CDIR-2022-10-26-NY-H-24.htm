    JOHN KATKO, Republican, of Camillus, NY; education: graduated from 
Bishop Ludden High School; B.A., Niagara University, cum laude, Niagara 
County, NY; J.D., Syracuse University College of Law, cum laude, 
Syracuse, NY; professional: Federal prosecutor for over 20 years, most 
recently as Assistant U.S. Attorney with the Northern District of New 
York; married: Robin, wife of over 33 years; three sons; committees: 
Homeland Security; Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 
114th Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
