    CLAY HIGGINS, Republican, of Lafayette, LA; born in New Orleans, 
LA, August 24, 1961; education: Covington High School, Covington, LA; 
attended Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, LA; professional: 
Lafayette City Marshal Deputy; Deputy Sheriff, St. Landry Parish; 
officer, Port Barre Police Department; reserve officer, Opelousas Police 
Department; manager, auto dealership; military: Military Police Corps, 
Louisiana National Guard; religion: Catholic; married: Rebecca; 
children: Daniella (deceased), Haley, Heather, and Joseph; committees: 
Homeland Security; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 115th Congress 
on December 10, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
