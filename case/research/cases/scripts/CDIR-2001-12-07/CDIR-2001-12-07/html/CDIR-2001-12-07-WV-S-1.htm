    ROBERT C. BYRD, Democrat, of Sophia, WV; born November 20, 1917; 
Baptist; married Erma Ora James; two daughters: Mrs. Mohammad (Mona 
Byrd) Fatemi and Mrs. Jon (Marjorie Byrd) Moore; six grandchildren: 
Erik, Darius and Fredrik Fatemi, and Michael (deceased), Mona and Mary 
Anne Moore; committees: chairman, Appropriations; Armed Services; 
Budget; Rules and Administration; elected as President pro tempore of 
the U.S. Senate on June 6, 2001; sworn in to the U.S. Senate on January 
3, 1959; reelected to each succeeding Senate term.
