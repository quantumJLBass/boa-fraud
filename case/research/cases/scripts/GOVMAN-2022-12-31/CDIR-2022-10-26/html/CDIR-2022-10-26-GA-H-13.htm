    DAVID SCOTT, Democrat, of Atlanta, GA; born in Aynor, SC, June 27, 
1945; education: Florida A&M University, Tallahassee, FL, graduated with 
honors, 1967; M.B.A., University of Pennsylvania Wharton School of 
Finance, graduated with honors, 1969; professional: businessman; owner 
and CEO, Dayn-Mark Advertising; public service: Georgia House of 
Representatives, 1974-82; Georgia State Senate, 1983-2002; married: 
Alfredia Aaron, 1969; children: Dayna and Marcye; committees: chair, 
Agriculture; Financial Services; elected to the 108th Congress on 
November 5, 2002; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
