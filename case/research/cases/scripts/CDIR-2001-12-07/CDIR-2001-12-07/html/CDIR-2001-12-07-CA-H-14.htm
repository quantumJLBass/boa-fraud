    ANNA G. ESHOO, Democrat, of Atherton, CA; born on December 13, 1942; 
attended, Canada College; San Mateo supervisor, 1983-92; has served on 
the House Committees on Science, Space, and Technology, and Merchant 
Marine and Fisheries; currently serves on the House Committee on 
Commerce, including its subcommittees on Telecommunications, Trade, and 
Consumer Protection, and Health and Environment; has served as a 
Democratic Regional Whip 

[[Page 25]]

since 1993; selected to co-chair the House 
Medical Technology Caucus, 1994; elected on November 3, 1992, to the 
103rd Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
