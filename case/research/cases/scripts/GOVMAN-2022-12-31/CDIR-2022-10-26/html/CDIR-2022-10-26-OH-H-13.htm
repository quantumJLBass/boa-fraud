    TIM RYAN, Democrat, of Howland, OH; born in Niles, OH, July 16, 
1973; education: B.S., Bowling Green University, 1995; J.D., University 
of New Hampshire School of Law (formerly Franklin Pierce Law Center), 
2000; professional: legislative aide, Washington, DC; Ohio State 
Senator, 2001-03; married: Andrea Ryan; committees: Appropriations; 
elected to the 108th Congress on November 5, 2002; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
