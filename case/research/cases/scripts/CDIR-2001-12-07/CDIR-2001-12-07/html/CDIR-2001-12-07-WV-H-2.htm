    SHELLEY MOORE CAPITO, Republican, of Charleston, WV; born on 
November 26, 1953, in Glen Dale, WV; education: B.S., Duke University; 
M.Ed., University of Virginia; profession: career counselor; employment: 
West Virginia State College; West Virginia Board of Regents; 
organizations: Community Council of Kanawha Valley; YWCA; West Virginia 
Interagency Council for Early Intervention; Habitat for Humanity; public 
service: elected to the West Virginia House of Delegates, 1996; 
reelected in 1998; awards: Coalition for a Tobacco-Free West Virginia 
Legislator of the Year; religion: Presbyterian; family: married to 
Charles L. Capito, Jr.; three children; elected to the 107th Congress on 
November 7, 2000.
