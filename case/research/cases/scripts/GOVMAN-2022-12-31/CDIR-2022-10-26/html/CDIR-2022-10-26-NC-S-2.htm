    THOM TILLIS, Republican, of Huntersville, NC; born in Jacksonville, 
FL, August 30, 1960; education: B.S., University of Maryland University 
College, 1997; professional: partner, IBM Global Business Services, 
2002-09; partner, PricewaterhouseCoopers, 1990-2002; public service: 
North Carolina State House Speaker, 2011-15, North Carolina State House, 
2009-15; religion: Catholic; married: Susan Tillis; children: one 
daughter, one son; committees: Armed Services; Banking, Housing, and 
Urban Affairs; Judiciary; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the U.S. Senate 
on November 4, 2014; reelected to the U.S. Senate on November 3, 2020.
