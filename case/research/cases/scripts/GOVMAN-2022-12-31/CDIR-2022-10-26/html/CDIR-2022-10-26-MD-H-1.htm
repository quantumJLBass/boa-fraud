    ANDY HARRIS, Republican, of Cockeysville, MD; born in Brooklyn, NY, 
January 25, 1957; education: B.S., Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, 
MD, 1977; M.D., Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, 1980; M.H.S., Johns 
Hopkins University, 1995; professional: anesthesiologist, as an 
associate professor of anesthesiology and critical care medicine; member 
of the Maryland State Senate, 1998-2010; Minority Whip, Maryland State 
Senate; military: Commander, Johns Hopkins Medical Naval Reserve Primus 
Unit P0605C; religion: Catholic; married: Nicole; five children; eight 
grandchildren; committees: Appropriations; elected to the 112th Congress 
on November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
