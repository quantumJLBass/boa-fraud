    STEVE SCALISE, Republican, of Jefferson, LA; born in New Orleans, 
LA, October 6, 1965; education: B.S., Louisiana State University, Baton 
Rouge, LA, 1989; professional: computer programmer; Louisiana House of 
Representatives, 1995-2007; Louisiana Senate, 2007-08; religion: 
Catholic; married to the former Jennifer Letulle; children: Madison and 
Harrison; elected, Chairman of the Republican Study Committee, 2013-14; 
elected, House Majority Whip, 2014-18; elected, House Republican Whip, 
2019-present; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected to the 110th 
Congress, by special election, on May 3, 2008; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
