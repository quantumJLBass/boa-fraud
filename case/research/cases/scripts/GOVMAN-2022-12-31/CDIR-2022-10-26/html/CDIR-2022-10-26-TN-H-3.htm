    CHUCK FLEISCHMANN, Republican, of Ooltewah, TN; born in New York 
City, NY, October 11, 1962; education: graduated from Elk Grove High 
School, Elk Grove Village, IL, 1980; B.A., political science, University 
of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, IL, 1983; J.D., University of Tennessee 
College of Law, Knoxville, TN, 1986; professional: attorney; small 
business owner; former president of the Chattanooga Bar Association, 
1996; former chairman of the Chattanooga Lawyers Pro Bono Committee; 
religion: Catholic; married: Brenda Fleischmann; three children; 
committees: Appropriations; elected to the 112th Congress on November 2, 
2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
