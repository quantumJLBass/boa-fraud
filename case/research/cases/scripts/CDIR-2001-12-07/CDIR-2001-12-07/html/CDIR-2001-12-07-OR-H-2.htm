    GREGORY PAUL WALDEN, Republican, of Hood River, OR; born on January 
10, 1957, in The Dalles, OR; married: Mylene Walden; one child: Anthony 
David Walden; education: graduated from the University of Oregon, 1981; 
B.S. in Journalism; owner, Columbia Gorge Broadcasters, Inc.; member, 
and Assistant Majority Leader, Oregon State Senate, 1995-97; member, 
Oregon State House of Representatives, 1989-95, and Majority Leader, 
1991-93; National Republican Legislators Association Legislator of the 
Year, 1993; Oregon Jaycees Outstanding Young Oregonian, 1991; member, 
Associated Oregon Industries; Oregon Health Sciences Foundation; Hood 
River Rotary Club; Hood River Elk's Club; National Federation of 
Independent Business; Hood River Chamber of Commerce; Hood River 
Memorial Hospital; Columbia Bancorp; committees: Energy and Commerce; 
Resources; subcommittees: Commerce, Trade, and Consumer Protection; 
Energy and Air Quality; Environment and Hazardous Materials; Water and 
Power; elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
