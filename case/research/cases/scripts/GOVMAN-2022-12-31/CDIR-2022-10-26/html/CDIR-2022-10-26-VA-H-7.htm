    ABIGAIL DAVIS SPANBERGER, Democrat, of Glen Allen, VA; born in Red 
Bank, Monmouth County, NJ, August 7, 1979; education: graduated from 
J.R. Tucker High School, Henrico, VA; United States Capitol Page School, 
1996; B.A., University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA, 2001; M.B.A., 
Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, 2002; professional: teacher; 
postal inspector, United States Postal Service; case officer, Central 
Intelligence Agency, 2006-14; consultant; married: Adam; children: 
three; caucuses: Blue Dog Coalition; Freshman Working Group on 
Addiction; Gun Violence Prevention Task Force; LGBT Equality Caucus; New 
Democrat Coalition; Rural Broadband Caucus; Servicewomen and Women 
Veterans Congressional Caucus; Women's Caucus; committees: Agriculture; 
Foreign Affairs; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
