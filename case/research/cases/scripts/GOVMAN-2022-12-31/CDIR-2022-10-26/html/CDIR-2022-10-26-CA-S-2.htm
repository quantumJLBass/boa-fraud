    ALEX PADILLA, Democrat, of Los Angeles, CA; born in Panorama City, 
CA,; March 22, 1973; education: B.S., mechanical engineering, 
Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, 1994; 
professional: member, Los Angeles City Council, 1999-2006, president,

[[Page 21]]

2001-06; California State Senator, District 20, 2006-14; State of 
California Secretary of State, 2015-21; married: Angela; children: three 
sons; committees: Budget; Environment and Public Works; Homeland 
Security and Governmental Affair; Judiciary; Rules and Administration; 
Joint Committee on Printing; appointed to the U.S. Senate on January 18, 
2021, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Kamala D. Harris, 
and took the oath of office on January 20, 2021.
