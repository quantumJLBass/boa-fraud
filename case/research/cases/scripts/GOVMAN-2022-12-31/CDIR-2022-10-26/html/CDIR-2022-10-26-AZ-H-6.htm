    DAVID SCHWEIKERT, Republican, of Fountain Hills, AZ; born March 3, 
1962; education: B.A., Arizona State University, Tempe, AZ, 1988; 
M.B.A., Arizona State University, Tempe,

[[Page 14]]

AZ, 2005; professional: business owner of a real estate company; 
realtor; financial consultant; member of the Arizona State House of 
Representatives, 1989-94; member of the Arizona State Board of 
Equalization, 1995-2003; former Treasurer, Maricopa County, AZ, 2004-06; 
religion: Catholic; married: Joyce Schweikert; committees: Ways and 
Means; Joint Economic Committee; elected to the 112th Congress on 
November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
