    TROY CARTER, Democrat, of New Orleans, LA; born in New Orleans, 
October 26, 1963; education: B.A., Xavier University, New Orleans, LA, 
1986; professional: entrepreneur; business consultant; member, Louisiana 
State House of Representatives, 1992-94; New Orleans, LA City Council, 
1994-2002; Louisiana State Senate, 2016-21; committees: Small Business; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th Congress, by 
special election, on April 24, 2021, to fill the vacancy caused by the 
resignation of Representative Cedric Richmond, and took the oath of 
office on May 11, 2021.
