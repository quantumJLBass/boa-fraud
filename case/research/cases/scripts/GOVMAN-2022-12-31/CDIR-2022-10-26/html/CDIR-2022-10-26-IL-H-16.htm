    ADAM KINZINGER, Republican, of Channahon, IL; born in Kankakee, IL, 
February 27, 1978; education: graduated, Normal Community West High 
School, 1996; B.S., Illinois State University, Norman, IL, 2000; 
professional: McLean County Board, 1998-2003; sales representative, STL 
Technologies, 2000-03; U.S. Air National Guard, 2003-present, current 
rank: Lieutenant Colonel; religion: Christian; married: Sofia Boza-
Holman; committees: Energy and Commerce; Foreign Affairs; elected to the 
112th Congress on November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
