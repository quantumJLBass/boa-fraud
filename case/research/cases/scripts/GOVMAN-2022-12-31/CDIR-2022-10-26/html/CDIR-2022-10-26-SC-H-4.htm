    WILLIAM R. TIMMONS IV, Republican, of Greenville, SC; born in 
Greenville, April 30, 1984; education: B.A., George Washington 
University, Washington, DC, 2006; M.A., University of South Carolina, 
Columbia, SC, 2009; J.D., University of South Carolina, 2010; 
professional: staff, U.S. Senator William H. Frist of Tennessee, 2006-
07; prosecutor, South Carolina's 13th

[[Page 243]]

Circuit Solicitor's Office, 2011-15; owner, Timmons & Company, LLC, 
2012-present; member, South Carolina State Senate, 2016-18; owner, Swamp 
Rabbit CrossFit, 2012-present; military: First Lieutenant, South 
Carolina Air National Guard, 2018-present; religion: Christian; 
committees: Financial Services; elected to the 116th Congress on 
November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
