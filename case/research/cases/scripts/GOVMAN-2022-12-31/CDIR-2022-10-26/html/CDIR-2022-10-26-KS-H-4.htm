    RON ESTES, Republican, of Wichita, KS; born in Topeka, KS; 
education: B.A., civil engineering, Tennessee Technological University, 
Cookeville, TN, 1973; M.B.A., Tennessee Technological University, 1983; 
professional: engineer; Sedgwick County (Kansas) Treasurer; Kansas State 
Treasurer; married: Susan Estes; children: Brent, Laura, and Grace; 
committees: Ways and Means; elected to the 115th Congress on April 11, 
2017, by special election, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation 
of U.S. Representative Mike Pompeo; reelected to the 116th Congress on 
November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
