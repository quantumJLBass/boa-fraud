    MICHAEL SAN NICOLAS, Democrat, of Dededo, Guam; born in Tamuning, 
Guam, January 30, 1981; education: B.A., University of Guam, Mangilao, 
Guam, 2004; professional: former high school teacher and financial 
adviser; Senator, Guam Legislature, 2013-18; committees: Financial 
Services; Natural Resources; elected to the 116th Congress on November 
6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
