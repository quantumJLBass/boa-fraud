    DARIN LaHOOD, Republican, of Peoria, IL; born in Peoria, July 5, 
1968; education: B.A., Loras College, Dubuque, IA, 1990; J.D., John 
Marshall Law School (now University of Illinois Chicago School of Law), 
Chicago, IL, 1997; professional: Illinois State Senate, 2011-15; Miller, 
Hall, & Triggs, 2006-15; Assistant U.S. Attorney, 2001-06; religion: 
Roman Catholic; married: Kristen; children: McKay, Lucas, and Teddy; 
committees: Ways and Means; Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence; 
elected to the 114th Congress, by special election, on September 10, 
2015, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of U.S. 
Representative Aaron Schock; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
