    ADAM SMITH, Democrat, of Tacoma, WA; born on June 15, 1965, in 
Washington, DC; graduated from Tyee High School, 1983; graduated from 
Fordham University, NY, 1987; law degree, University of Washington, 
1990; admitted to the Washington bar in 1991; prosecutor for the city of 
Seattle; Washington State Senate, 1990-96; member, Kent Drinking Driver 
Task Force; board member, Judson Park Retirement Home; committees: Armed 
Services; Resources; married Sara Smith, 1993; elected to the 105th 
Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
