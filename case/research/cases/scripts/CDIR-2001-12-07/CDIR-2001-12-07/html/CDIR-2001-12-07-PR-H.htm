    ANIBAL ACEVEDO-VILA, Popular Democratic Party, of Puerto Rico; born 
on February 13, 1962, in Hato Rey, PR; education: Political Science 
degree, University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras Campus, 1982; law degree, 
University of Puerto Rico, 1985; profession: Attorney; served as 
Legislative Advisor for Puerto Rico Gov. Rafael Hernandez Colon, 1989-
1992; public service: elected President of the Popular Democratic Party; 
elected to two terms in the Puerto Rico House of Representatives; 
elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
