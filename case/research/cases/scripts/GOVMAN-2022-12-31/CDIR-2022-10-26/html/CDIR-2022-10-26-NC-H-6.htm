    KATHY E. MANNING, Democrat, of Greensboro, NC; born in Detroit, MI, 
December 3, 1956; education: B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 
1978; J.D., University of Michigan Law School, Ann Arbor, MI, 1981; 
professional: partner, Smith Moore Leatherwood, LLP, Greensboro, NC, 
1987-2003; partner, Manning and Associates, PLLC, 2004-present; chair, 
board of trustees, Jewish Federations of North America, 2009-12, New 
York, NY; caucuses: Bipartisan Historically Black Colleges and 
Universities Caucus; Equality Caucus; New Democrats Coalition; Pro-
Choice Caucus; committees: Education and Labor; Foreign Affairs; elected 
to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
