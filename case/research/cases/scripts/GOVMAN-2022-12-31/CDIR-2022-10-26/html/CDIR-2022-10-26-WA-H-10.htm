    MARILYN STRICKLAND, Democrat, of Tacoma, WA; born in Seoul, South 
Korea, September 25, 1962; education: B.A., University of Washington, 
Seattle, WA, 1984; M.B.A., Clark-Atlanta University, Atlanta, GA, 1992; 
professional: marketing communications executive; member, Tacoma City 
Council, 2008-09; Mayor, Tacoma, 2010-17; CEO, Seattle Metropolitan 
Chamber of Commerce, 2018-20; spouse: Patrick Erwin; committees: Armed 
Services; Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th 
Congress on November 3, 2020.
