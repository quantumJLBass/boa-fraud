    KAT CAMMACK, Republican, of Gainesville, FL; born in Denver, CO, 
February 16, 1988; education: B.A., Metropolitan State University of 
Denver, Denver, CO, 2011; M.S., Naval War College, Newport, RI, 2018; 
professional: small business owner; staff, U.S. Representative Ted Yoho 
of Florida, 2012-20; married: Matt Harrison; caucuses: co-chair, Pro-
Life Caucus; co-vice chair, Congressional Women's Caucus; Congressional 
Sportsmen's Caucus; General Aviation Caucus; Law Enforcement Caucus; 
Rural Broadband Caucus; Soils Caucus; Working Forests Caucus; 
committees: Agriculture; Homeland Security; elected to the 117th 
Congress on November 3, 2020.
