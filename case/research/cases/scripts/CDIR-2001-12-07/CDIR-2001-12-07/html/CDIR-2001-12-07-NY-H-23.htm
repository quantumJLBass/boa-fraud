    SHERWOOD L. BOEHLERT, Republican, of New Hartford, NY; born in 
Utica, NY, on September 28, 1936; education: graduated from Whitesboro 
Central High School; Utica College, B.A., 1961; military service: served 
in the U.S. Army, 1956-58; employment: 1961-64, manager of Public 
Relations, Wyandotte Chemicals Corporation; 1964-72, chief of staff for 
Congressman Alexander Pirnie; 1973-79, chief of staff for Congressman 
Donald J. Mitchell; elected 1979, Oneida County Executive; member: board 
of directors, Utica College Foundation; St. John the Evangelist Church, 
New Hartford; numerous awards including honorary doctoral degrees from 
Syracuse University, Colgate University and Cazenovia College; married: 
the former Marianne Willey; four children; committees: chairman, 
Science; member, Transportation and Infrastructure; House Permanent 
Select Committee on Intelligence; elected on November 2, 1982 to the 
98th Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
