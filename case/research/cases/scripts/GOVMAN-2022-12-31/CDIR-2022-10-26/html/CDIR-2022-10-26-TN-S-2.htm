    BILL HAGERTY, Jr., Republican, of Nashville, TN; born in Gallatin, 
TN, August 14, 1959; education: B.A., Vanderbilt University, Nashville, 
TN, 1981; J.D., Vanderbilt University, Nashville, TN, 1984; 
professional: management consultant; White House Fellow and staff member 
during the George H. W. Bush administration; co-founder and managing 
director, private equity investment firm; commissioner of the Tennessee 
Department of Economic and Community Development 2011-14; U.S. 
Ambassador to Japan 2017-19; committees: Appropriations; Banking, 
Housing, and Urban Affairs; Foreign Relations; Rules and Administration; 
elected to the U.S. Senate on November 3, 2020.
