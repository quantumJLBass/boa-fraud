    ANGIE CRAIG, Democrat, of Eagan, MN; born in West Helena, AR, 
February 14, 1972; education: B.A., journalism, University of Memphis, 
Memphis, TN, 1994; professional: St. Jude Medical, human resources and 
communications, 2005-17; religion: Lutheran; family: married to Cheryl 
Greene, with four sons; committees: Agriculture; Energy and Commerce; 
Small Business; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
