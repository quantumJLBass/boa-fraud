    MICHAEL CLOUD, Republican, of Victoria, TX; born in Baton Rouge, 
LA, May 13, 1975; education: graduated from Miamisburg High School, 
Miamisburg, OH, 1993; B.S., Oral Roberts University, Tulsa, OK, 1997; 
professional: communications director; business owner; committees: 
Agriculture; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 115th Congress, by 
special election, on June 30, 2018, to fill the vacancy caused by the 
resignation of U.S. Representative Blake Farenthold; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
