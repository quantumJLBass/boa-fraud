    BRENDAN F. BOYLE, Democrat, of Philadelphia, PA; born in the Olney 
section of Philadelphia, February 6, 1977; education: graduated from the 
Cardinal Dougherty High School in Philadelphia, 1995; B.A., University 
of Notre Dame, South Bend, IN, 1999; M.P.P., John F. Kennedy School of 
Government, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 2005; professional: 
representative, Pennsylvania House of Representatives, 170th District, 
2009-15; married: Jennifer Boyle; child: Abigail; committees: Budget; 
Ways and Means; elected to represent the 13th District in the 114th 
Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to the 115th Congress on 
November 8, 2016; elected to represent the 2nd District in the 116th 
Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
