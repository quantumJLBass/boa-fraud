    DOUG LaMALFA, Republican, of Richvale, CA; born in Oroville, CA, 
July 2, 1960; education: graduated from Oroville High School; B.S., 
California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, CA, 1982; 
professional: rice farmer; California State Assemblyman, 2002-08; 
California State Senator, 2010-12; married: Jill; children: four; 
caucuses: Border Security Caucus; Cement Caucus; Congressional 
Sportsmen's Caucus; Congressional Western Caucus; Congressional Wine 
Caucus; National Guard and Reserve Components Caucus; Natural Gas 
Caucus; PORTS Caucus; Prayer Caucus; Republican Study Committee; Rice 
Caucus; Sikh Caucus; Small Business Caucus; Values Action Team; 
committees: Agriculture; Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to 
the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
