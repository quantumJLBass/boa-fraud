    RICHARD W. POMBO, Republican, of Tracy, CA; born in Tracy, CA, on 
January 8, 1961; attended California State University at Pomona; 
rancher; Tracy, CA, City Councilman, 1990-91; cofounder, San Joaquin 
County Citizens Land Alliance; member, Tracy Rotary Club; married to 
Annette Pombo since 1983; children: Richard Jr., Rena, and Rachel; 
committees: Agriculture; Resources; Transportation and Infrastructure; 
subcommittee assignments include areas covering specialty crops, 
domestic and international agricultural marketing, livestock, farm 
credit, water policy, and mining; elected November 3, 1992, to the 103rd 
Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
