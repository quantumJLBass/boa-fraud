    TRACEY MANN, Republican, of Salina, KS; born in Quinter, KS, 
December 17, 1976; education: B.A., Kansas State University, Manhattan, 
KS, 2000; professional: commercial real estate broker; appointed Kansas 
state lieutenant governor, 2018-19; religion: Christian; family: wife; 
children: four; committees: Agriculture; Veterans' Affairs; elected to 
the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
