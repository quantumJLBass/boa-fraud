    ANN McLANE KUSTER, Democrat, of Hopkinton, NH; born in Concord, NH, 
September 5, 1956; education: B.A., Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH, 
1978; J.D., Georgetown University Law Center, Washington, DC, 1984; 
professional: consultant and owner, Newfound Strategies LLC; lawyer and 
partner, Rath, Young and Pignatelli; married: Brad Kuster; children: 
Zach and Travis; committees: Agriculture; Energy and Commerce; elected 
to the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
