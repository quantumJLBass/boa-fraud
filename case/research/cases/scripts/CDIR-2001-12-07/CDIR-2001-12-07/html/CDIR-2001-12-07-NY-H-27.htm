    THOMAS M. REYNOLDS, Republican, of Springville, NY; born in 
Springville, NY, on September 3, 1950; attended Kent State University; 
Erie County legislator, 1982-88; New York State Assembly, 1988-98; 
former director, Better Business Bureau, Cooperative Extension and 
Central Referral Service; member: House Administration and Rules 
Committees; he and his wife, Donna, are the parents of four children; 
elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
