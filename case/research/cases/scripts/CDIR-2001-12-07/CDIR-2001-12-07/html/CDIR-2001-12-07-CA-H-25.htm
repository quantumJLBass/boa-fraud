    HOWARD P. (BUCK) McKEON, Republican, of Santa Clarita, CA; born in 
Los Angeles, CA, on September 9, 1938; education: graduated, Verdugo 
Hills High School, Tujunga, CA; B.S., Brigham Young University; owner, 
Howard and Phil's Western Wear; mayor and city councilman, Santa 
Clarita, 1987-92; member: board of directors, Canyon Country Chamber of 
Commerce; California Republican State Central Committee; advisory 
council, Boy Scouts of America; president and trustee, William S. Hart 
School District, 1979-87; chairman and director, Henry Mayo Newhall 
Memorial Hospital, 1983-87; chairman and founding director, Valencia 
National Bank, 1987-92; honorary chairman, Red Cross Community Support 
Campaign, 1992; honorary chairman, Leukemia Society Celebrity Program, 
1990 and 1994; president, Republican Freshman Class of the 103rd 
Congress; married: to the former Patricia Kunz, 1962; children: Tamara, 
Howard D., John Matthew, Kimberly, David Owen, and Tricia; elected on 
November 3, 1992, to the 103rd Congress; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
