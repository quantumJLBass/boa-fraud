    GREGORY F. MURPHY, M.D., Republican, of Greenville, NC; born in 
1963; education: B.S., pre-medical studies, religion, Davidson College, 
Davidson, NC, 1985; M.D., University of North Carolina School of 
Medicine, Chapel Hill, NC, 1989; Chief of Staff, Vidant Medical Center, 
3-year term; Division Chief of Urology, Department of Surgery, East 
Carolina University School of Medicine, Greenville, NC; president and 
senior management partner, Eastern Urological Associates, P.A., 
Greenville, NC; married: Wendy; three children; committees: Ways and 
Means; elected to the 116th Congress, by special election, on September 
10, 2019, to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Walter B. Jones; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
