    ADAM SMITH, Democrat, of Tacoma, WA; born in Washington, DC, June 
15, 1965; education: graduated, Tyee High School, 1983; Fordham 
University, Bronx, NY, 1987; law degree, University of Washington, 1990; 
admitted to the Washington Bar in 1991; professional: Prosecutor for the 
city of Seattle; Washington State Senate, 1990-96; married: Sara Smith, 
1993; committees: chair, Armed Services; elected to the 105th Congress 
on November 5, 1996; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
