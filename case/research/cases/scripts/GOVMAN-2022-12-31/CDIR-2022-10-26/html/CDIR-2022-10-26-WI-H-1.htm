    BRYAN STEIL, Republican, of Janesville, WI; born in Janesville, 
March 3, 1981; education: Joseph A. Craig High School; B.S., business 
administration, Georgetown University, Washington, DC, 2003; J.D., 
University of Wisconsin School of Law, Madison, WI, 2007; professional: 
legal counsel, Regal Beloit; attorney for Milton, WI-based plastics 
manufacturer Charter NEX Film; member, University of Wisconsin Board of 
Regents, 2016-18; committees: Financial Services; House Administration; 
elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 
117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
