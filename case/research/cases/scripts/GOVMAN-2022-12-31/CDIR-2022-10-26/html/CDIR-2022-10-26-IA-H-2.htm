    MARIANNETTE MILLER-MEEKS, Republican, of Ottumwa, IA; born in 
Herlong, CA, September 6, 1955; education: B.S.N., Texas Christian 
University, Fort Worth, TX, 1976; M.S., University of Southern 
California, Los Angeles, CA, 1980; M.D., University of Texas Science 
Center, San Antonio, TX, 1986; professional: United States Army; United 
States Army Reserves; assistant professor, University of Michigan, 1991-
94; assistant professor, University of Iowa, 1994-97; ophthalmologist; 
director, Iowa Department of Public Health, 2010-13; member, Iowa State 
Senate, 2019-20; family: husband, Curt Meeks; children: Jonathon Miller-
Meeks and Taylor Miller-Meeks; committees: Education and Labor; Homeland 
Security; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress on November 
3, 2020.
