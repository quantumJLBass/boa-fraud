    PETER G. FITZGERALD, Republican, of Inverness, IL; born in Elgin, 
IL, on October 20, 1960; graduated from Portsmouth Abbey, 1978; B.A., 
Dartmouth College, 1982; J.D., University of Michigan School of Law, 
1986; corporate attorney; State Senator in Illinois General Assembly, 
1992-98; married: Nina; one child: Jake; committees: Agriculture, 
Nutrition, and Forestry; Commerce, Science, and Transportation; Special 
Committee on Aging; Small Business; elected to the U.S. Senate on 
November 3, 1998.
