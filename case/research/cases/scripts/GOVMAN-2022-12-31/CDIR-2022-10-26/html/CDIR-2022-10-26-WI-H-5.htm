    SCOTT FITZGERALD, Republican, of Juneau, WI; born in Chicago, IL, 
November 16, 1963; education: B.S., journalism, University of Wisconsin-
Oshkosh, WI, 1985; professional: U.S. Army Reserve, 1981-2009; owner and 
publisher of Dodge County Independent News, Juneau, WI, 1990-96; Dodge 
County Republican Chairman, 1992-94; Senator, Wisconsin State Senate, 
1994-2020 (Majority Leader: 2004, 2011-12, 2013-20; Minority Leader: 
2007, 2009, 2012-13); married: wife, Lisa; three sons: Scott, Brennan, 
and Connor; committees: Education and Labor; Judiciary; Small Business; 
elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
