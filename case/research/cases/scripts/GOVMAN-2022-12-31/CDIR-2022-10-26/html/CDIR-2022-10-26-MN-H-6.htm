    TOM EMMER, Republican, of Delano, MN; born in South Bend, IN, March 
3, 1961; education: B.A., political science, University of Alaska-
Fairbanks, Fairbanks, AK, 1984; J.D., William Mitchell College of Law, 
St. Paul, MN, 1988; professional: practiced insurance, banking, and 
equity law through his own practice; served in the Minnesota House of 
Representatives

[[Page 148]]

from 2004-08; was a radio host on Twin Cities News Talk AM 1130; 
married: Jacquie; children: sons, Thomas Earl III ``Tripp'', Jack, 
Bobby, Joey, Billy, and Johnny; daughter, Katie; House Republican 
Steering Committee; committees: Financial Services; elected to the 114th 
Congress on November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
