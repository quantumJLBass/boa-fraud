    JOHN A. YARMUTH, Democrat, of Louisville, KY; born in Louisville, 
November 4, 1947; education: Atherton High School, Louisville, KY, 1965; 
B.A., Yale University, New Haven, CT, 1969; professional: legislative 
aide for Kentucky Senator Marlow Cook, 1971-74; publisher, Louisville 
Today Magazine, 1976-82; Associate Vice President of University 
Relations at the University of Louisville, 1983-86; vice president of a 
local healthcare firm, 1986-90; founder, editor, and writer, LEO 
Newsweekly, 1990-2005; television host and commentator, 2003-05; awards: 
2007 Spirit of Enterprise Award; Louisville Alzheimer's Association 
Person of the Year; named Outstanding New Member of Congress by the 
Committee for Education and Funding; 16 Metro Louisville Journalism 
Awards for editorial and column writing; married: Cathy Yarmuth, 1981; 
child: Aaron; committees: chair, Budget; elected to the 110th Congress 
on November 7, 2006; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
