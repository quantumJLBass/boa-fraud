    JERRY L. CARL, Republican, of Mobile, AL; born in Mobile, AL, June 
17, 1958; education: graduated from Sylacauga High School, Sylacauga, 
AL, 1977; attended Lake City Community College, Lake City, FL; 
professional: business owner; entrepreneur; sales representative; 
commissioner, Mobile County, AL, 2012-20; president, Mobile County, AL, 
2015-16 and 2019-20; religion: Baptist; wife: Tina; children: Dwight, 
Dallas, and Sarah; committees: Armed Services; Natural Resources; 
elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
