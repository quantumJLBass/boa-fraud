    CONNIE CONWAY, Republican, of Tulare, CA; born in Bakersfield, CA, 
September 25, 1950; education: graduated from San Joaquin Memorial High 
School, Bakersfield, CA; attended College of the Sequoias, Visalia, CA; 
attended California State University, Fresno, CA; professional: hospital 
wellness coordinator; marketing director; corporate district manager; 
director, Tulare, CA redevelopment agency, 1996-2001; member, Tulare 
County, CA board of supervisors, 2001-08 (chair, 2005 and 2008); 
president, California state association of counties, 2006; member of the 
California state assembly, 2008-14 (minority leader, 2010-14); executive 
director, United States Farm Service Agency, United States Department of 
Agriculture, 2019-21;

[[Page 32]]

member, California community college board of governors; committees: 
Natural Resources. Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress, by 
special election, on June 7, 2022, to fill the vacancy caused by the 
resignation of United States Representative Devin Nunes.
