    BRIAN BABIN, Republican, of Woodville, TX; born in Port Arthur, TX, 
March 23, 1948; education: B.S., Lamar University, Beaumont, TX; D.D.S., 
University of Texas; professional: dentist; Mayor of Woodville, 1982-84; 
Woodville City Councilman, 1984-89; Woodville Independent School Board, 
1992-95; director of Tyler County Chamber of Commerce; president of 
Texas State Board of Dental Examiners, 1981-87; Deep East Texas Council 
of Governments member, 1982-84; Texas Historical Commission, 1989-95; 
Appointee to Lower Neches Valley Authority, 1999-2014; caucuses: co-
chairman, Border Security Caucus; co-chairman, Congressional I-14 
Caucus; co-chairman, Congressional Texas Maritime Caucus; Congressional 
Aerospace Caucus; Congressional Election Integrity Caucus; Congressional 
Sportsmen's Caucus; GOP Doctors Caucus; Military Veterans Caucus; Pro 
Life Caucus; Western Caucus; committees: Science, Space, and Technology; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 114th Congress on 
November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
