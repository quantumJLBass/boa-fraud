    ANTONIO CARDENAS, Democrat, of San Fernando Valley, CA; born in 
Pacoima, CA, March 31, 1963; education: B.A., University of California 
at Santa Barbara, 1986; professional: businessman; public service: 
California State Assembly, 1996-2002; Los Angeles City Council, 2002-13; 
religion: Christian; family: married to Norma Sanchez; children: Andres, 
Alina, Vanessa, and Cristian; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected 
to the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
