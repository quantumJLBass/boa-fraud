    JOSH HARDER, Democrat, of Turlock, CA; born in Turlock, Stanislaus 
County, CA, August 1, 1986; education: graduated from Modesto Public 
High School, Modesto, CA; B.A., Stanford University, Stanford, CA; 
M.B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA; M.P.P., Harvard University, 
Cambridge, MA; professional: entrepreneur; consultant; educator; 
caucuses: Assyrian Caucus; Portuguese-American Caucus; committees: 
Agriculture; Appropriations; elected to the 116th Congress on November 
6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
