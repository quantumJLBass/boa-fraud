    MONDAIRE JONES, Democrat, of Westchester County, NY; born in Nyack, 
NY, May 18, 1987; education: B.A., Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 
2009; J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 2013; professional: 
staff, U.S. Department of Justice, 2013-16; lawyer, private practice, 
Westchester County, NY attorney, 2018-19; committees: Education and 
Labor; Ethics; Judiciary; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 
2020.
