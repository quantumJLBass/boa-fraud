    JAKE ELLZEY, Republican, of Midlothian, TX; born in Perryton TX, 
January 24, 1970; education: B.S., political science, U.S. Naval 
Academy, Annapolis, MD, 1992; professional: pilot; 20-year Navy veteran, 
retired as Commander; Commissioner, Texas Veterans Commission, 2012-
present; consultant, BGI, Limited Liability Company, 2012-13; Chief 
Executive Officer, HoldFast Headquarters, 2015-17; Representative, 
District 10, Texas State House of Representatives, Austin, TX, 2021; 
religion: Christian; family: married to Shelby Ellzey; two children; 
committees: Science, Space, and Technology; Veterans' Affairs; elected 
to the 117th Congress on July 27, 2021 by special election; sworn in on 
July 30, 2021.
