    BOB GOOD, Republican, of Evington, VA; born in Pennsylvania, 
September 11, 1965; education: B.S., finance, Liberty University, 
Lynchburg, VA, 1988; M.B.A., Liberty University, 2010; professional: 
branch manager, area training director and district manager, 
CitiFinancial, 1988-2005; Athletic Department, Liberty University, 
Lynchburg, VA, 2005-20; member, Campbell County Board of Supervisors, 
2015-19; religion: Christian; married: wife: Tracey; three grown 
children: Patrick, Sydney, and Connor; committees: Budget; Education and 
Labor; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
