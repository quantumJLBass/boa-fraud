    JUDY BIGGERT, Republican, of Hinsdale, IL; born in Chicago, IL, on 
August 15, 1937; education: graduated from New Trier High School, 1955; 
B.A., Stanford University, 1959; J.D., Northwestern University School of 
Law; employment: attorney, 1975-present; Illinois House of 
Representatives (81st District), 1993-98; Assembly committees: 
Judiciary; Human Services; Appropriations; Labor and Commerce; and 
Conflict of Interest; Assistant House Republican Leader, 1995-99; has 
served on numerous local civic and community organizations and groups; 
committees: Government Reform; Science; Financial Services; married: 
Rody P. Biggert; children: Courtney, Alison, Rody, and Adrienne; 
Episcopalian; elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 107th 
Congress.
