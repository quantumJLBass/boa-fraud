    MICHAEL T. McCAUL, Republican, of Austin, TX; born in Dallas, TX, 
January 14, 1962; education: B.S., Trinity University, San Antonio, TX, 
1984; J.D., St. Mary's University, San Antonio, TX, 1987; professional: 
lawyer, private practice; Deputy Attorney General, Office of Texas State 
Attorney General; committees: ranking member, Foreign Affairs; Homeland 
Security; elected to the 109th Congress on November 2, 2004; reelected 
to each succeeding Congress.
