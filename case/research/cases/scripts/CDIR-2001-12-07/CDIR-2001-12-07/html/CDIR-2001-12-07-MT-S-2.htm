    CONRAD BURNS, Republican, of Billings, MT; born in Gallatin, MO, on 
January 25, 1935; graduated, Gallatin High School, 1952; attended 
University of Missouri, Columbia, 1953-54; served, U.S. Marine Corps, 
corporal, 1955-57; farm broadcaster and auctioneer; county commissioner, 
Yellowstone County, 1986; member: Rotary, American Legion, National 
Association of Farm Broadcasters, American Association of Farm 
Broadcasters, Atonement Lutheran Church; married to the former Phyllis 
Kuhlmann; two children: Keely and Garrett; committees: Appropriations; 
Commerce, Science, and Transportation; Energy and Natural Resources; 
Small Business and Entrepreneurship; Special Committee on Aging; elected 
to the U.S. Senate on November 8, 1988; reelected to each succeeding 
Senate term.
