    JEFF DUNCAN, Republican, of Laurens, SC; born in Greenville, SC, 
January 7, 1966; education: B.A., political science, Clemson University, 
Clemson, SC, 1988; professional: small business owner; public service: 
South Carolina House of Representatives, 2002-10; religion: Southern 
Baptist, attends Clinton First Baptist Church; married: Melody; 
children: Graham, John Philip, and Parker; committees: Energy and 
Commerce; elected to the 112th Congress on November 2, 2010; reelected 
to each succeeding Congress.
