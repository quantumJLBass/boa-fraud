    PATRICK RYAN, Democrat, of Gardiner, NY; born in Kingston, Ulster 
County, NY, March 28, 1982; education: graduated from Kingston High 
School, Kingston, NY, 2000; B.S., United States Military Academy, West 
Point, NY, 2004; M.A., Georgetown University, Washington, DC, 2013; 
professional: United States Army, 2004-09; business executive; business 
owner; county executive, 2019-22; committees: Armed Services; elected to 
the 117th Congress, by special election, on August 23, 2022, to fill the 
vacancy created by the resignation of U.S. Representative Antonio 
Delgado.
