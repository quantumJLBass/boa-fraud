    DARREN SOTO, Democrat, of Kissimmee, FL; born in Ringwood, NJ, 
February 25, 1978; education: B.A., economics, Rutgers University, New 
Brunswick, NJ, 2000; J.D., George Washington University Law School, 
Washington, DC, 2004; professional: founder of Darren Soto Law Firm; 
served for 4 years in the Florida Senate; served for 5 years in the 
Florida House of Representatives; former treasurer of the Orange County 
Democrats; former vice president of communications for the Orange County 
Young Democrats; awards: Champion of Northern Everglades, the Audubon 
Society of Florida; religion: Roman Catholic; married: Amanda Soto; 
leadership: former Assistant Regional Whip, Democratic Steering and 
Policy; caucuses: whip, Congressional Hispanic Caucus; chair, 
Congressional #FutureForum Caucus; Congenital Heart Caucus; New Democrat 
Coalition; Problem Solvers Caucus; committees: Energy and Commerce; 
Natural Resources; elected to the 115th Congress on November 8, 2016; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
