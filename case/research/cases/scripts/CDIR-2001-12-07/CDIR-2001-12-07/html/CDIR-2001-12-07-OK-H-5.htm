    ERNEST J. ISTOOK, Jr., Republican, of Oklahoma City, OK; born in 
Fort Worth, TX, on February 11, 1950; education: graduated, Castleberry 
High School, Ft. Worth, 1967; B.A., Baylor University, 1971; J.D., 
Oklahoma City University, 1976; emploment: attorney; admitted to the 
Oklahoma bar, 1977; reporter, WKY, KOMA, 1972-77; city councilman, Warr 
Acres, 1982-86; library board chairman, Oklahoma City, 1985-86; 
director, Warr Acres Chamber of Commerce, 1986-92; Oklahoma State House 
of Representatives, 1986-92; married: the former Judy Bills, 1973; 
children: Butch, Chad, Amy, Diana, and Emily; committees: 
Appropriations; elected on November 3, 1992 to the 103rd Congress; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
