    RANDY FEENSTRA, Republican, of Hull, IA; born in Hull, January 14, 
1969; education: B.A., Dordt College, Sioux Center, IA, 1991; M.P.A., 
Iowa State University, Ames, IA; professional: business manager; bank 
insurance manager; faculty, Dordt College, Sioux Center, IA; city 
administrator, Hull, IA, 1999-2006; treasurer, Sioux Center, IA, 2006-
08; member, Iowa State Senate, 2009-20; religion: Protestant Christian; 
family: wife Lynette; children: Taylor, Erika, Dawson, and Savannah; 
committees: Agriculture; Budget; Science, Space, and Technology; elected 
to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
