    MATTHEW M. ROSENDALE, Sr., Republican, of Glendive, MT; born in 
Baltimore, MD, July 7, 1960; education: graduated from Queen Anne's 
County High School, Centreville, MD, 1978; attended Chesapeake College, 
Wye Mills, MD; professional: real estate sales and development; land 
management; Montana State Representative, 2011-13; Montana State 
Senator, 2013-17, Majority Leader, 2015; Montana State Auditor / 
Commissioner of Securities and Insurance, 2017-21; religion: Catholic; 
married: wife, Jean; three children; committees: Natural Resources; 
Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
