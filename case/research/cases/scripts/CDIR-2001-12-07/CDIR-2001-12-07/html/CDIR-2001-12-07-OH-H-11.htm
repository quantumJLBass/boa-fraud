    STEPHANIE TUBBS JONES, Democrat, of Cleveland, OH; born in 
Cleveland, OH, on September 10, 1949; B.A., Case Western Reserve 
University, 1971; J.D., Case Western Reserve University, 1974; 
Prosecutor, Cuyahoga County, OH; Judge of Common Pleas and Municipal 
Courts; Baptist; married to Mervyn; one child; committees: Financial 
Services; Small Business; elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to 
the 107th Congress.
