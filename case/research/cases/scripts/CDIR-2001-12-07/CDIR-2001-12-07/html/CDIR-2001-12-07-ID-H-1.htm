    C.L. (BUTCH) OTTER, Republican, of Star, ID; born on May 3, 1942, in 
Caldwell, ID; education: College of Idaho, B.A., Political Science; 
Mindanao State University in the Philippines, Honorary Doctorate; 
employment: businessman; J.R. Simplot Co.; Kyn Ten Oil Drilling Co.; 
organizations: Idaho International Trade Council; Elks Club; Ducks 
Unlimited; National Rifle Association; Idaho Agricultural Leadership 
Council; public service: Idaho Army National Guard, 1968-73; Idaho House 
of Representatives, 1972-76; Lt. Governor of Idaho, 1987-2000; elected 
to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
