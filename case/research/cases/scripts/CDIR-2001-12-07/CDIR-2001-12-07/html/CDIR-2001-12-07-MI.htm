November 6, 1990, and November 5, 1996.

                             THIRD DISTRICT

    VERNON J. EHLERS, Republican, of Grand Rapids, MI; born on February 
6, 1934, in Pipestone, MN; educated at home by his parents; attended 
Calvin College; undergraduate degree in physics and Ph.D. in nuclear

commissions; member, Michigan House and Senate; first research physicist

engineering, and technology education; as member of House Administration 
Committee, guided the program to revamp the House computer

committees: chairman, Joint Committee of Congress on the Library; 
Education and the Workforce; House Administration; Science; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 103d Congress by 
special election, December 1993; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
