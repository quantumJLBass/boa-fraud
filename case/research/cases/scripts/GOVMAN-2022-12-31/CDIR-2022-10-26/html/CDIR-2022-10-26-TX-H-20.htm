    JOAQUIN CASTRO, Democrat, of San Antonio, TX; born in San Antonio, 
September 16, 1974; education: Thomas Jefferson High School, 1992; B.A., 
Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 1996; J.D., Harvard University, 
Cambridge, MA, 2000; professional: attorney; law instructor; religion: 
Catholic; family: wife, Anna Flores; caucuses: Congressional Caucus on 
ASEAN; Congressional Hispanic Caucus; Congressional Pre-K Caucus; New 
Democrat Coalition; U.S.-Japan Caucus; committees: Education and Labor; 
Foreign Affairs; Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence; elected to 
the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
