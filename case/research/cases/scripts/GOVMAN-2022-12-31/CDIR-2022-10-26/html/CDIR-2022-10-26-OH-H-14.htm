    DAVID JOYCE, Republican, of Geauga, OH; born in Cleveland, OH, 
March 17, 1957; education: B.S., University of Dayton, Dayton, OH, 1979; 
J.D., University of Dayton, 1982; professional: prosecuting attorney, 
Geauga County, 1988-2012; married: Kelly Joyce; children: Trenton, 
Keighle, and Bridey; committees: Appropriations; Ethics; elected to the 
113th Congress on November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
