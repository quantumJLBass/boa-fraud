    JOHN T. DOOLITTLE, Republican, of Rocklin, CA; born in Glendale, CA, 
October 30, 1950; education: graduated Cupertino High School, Cupertino, 
CA, 1968; University of California at Santa Cruz, 1972; University of 
the Pacific, McGeorge School of Law, 1978; employment: lawyer; member: 
California bar; elected to the California State Senate, 1980; reelected 
1984 and 1988; served as chairman of the Senate Republican Caucus, May 
1987-April 1990; married: the former Julia Harlow, 1979; children: John, 
Jr. and Courtney Doolittle; elected to the 102nd Congress, November 6, 
1990; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
