    GWENDOLYNNE S. "GWEN" MOORE, Democrat, of Milwaukee, WI; born in 
Racine, WI, April 18, 1951; education: graduated, Northern Division High 
School, Milwaukee, WI, 1969; B.A., Marquette University, Milwaukee, WI, 
1978; professional: housing officer, Wisconsin Housing and Development 
Authority; member: Wisconsin State Assembly, 1989-92; Wisconsin State 
Senate, 1993-2004; president pro tempore, 1997-98; family: three 
children; committees: Science, Space, and Technology; Ways and Means; 
elected to the 109th Congress on November 2, 2004; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
