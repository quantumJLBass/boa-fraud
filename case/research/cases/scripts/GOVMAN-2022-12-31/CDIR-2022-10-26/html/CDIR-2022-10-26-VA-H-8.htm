    DONALD S. BEYER, Jr., Democrat, of Alexandria, VA; born in the Free 
Territory of Trieste, June 20, 1950; education: B.A., Williams College, 
Williamstown, MA, 1972; professional: Ambassador to Switzerland and 
Liechtenstein, 2009-13; 36th Lieutenant Governor of Virginia, 1990-98; 
co-founder of the Northern Virginia Technology Council; former chair of 
Jobs for Virginia Graduates; served on Board of the DC Campaign to 
Prevent Teen Pregnancy; former chair of the Virginia Economic Recovery 
Commission; spouse: Megan; children: Don, Stephanie, Clara, and Grace; 
caucuses: Congressional Progressive Caucus; New Democrat Coalition; 
committees: chair, Joint Economic Committee; Science, Space, and 
Technology; Ways and Means; elected to the 114th Congress on November 4, 
2014; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
