    A. DONALD McEACHIN, Democrat, of Henrico County, VA; born in 
Nuremberg, Germany, October 10, 1961; education: B.S., political 
history, American University, Washington, DC, 1982; J.D., University of 
Virginia, Charlottesville, VA; M.Div., Virginia Union University, 
Richmond, VA, 2008; religion: Episcopal; married: Colette McEachin; 
three adult children; caucuses: vice chair, Sustainable Energy and 
Environment Coalition (SEEC); Black Maternal Health Caucus; Chesapeake 
Bay Watershed Caucus; Congressional Black Caucus; Congressional PORTS 
Caucus; Congressional Ship-Building Caucus; House Democratic Caucus; 
House Pro-

[[Page 282]]

Choice Caucus; LGBT Equality Caucus; New Democrat Coalition (NDC); 
taskforces: co-founder, United for Climate Environmental Justice 
Congressional Taskforce; co-chair, CBC Energy, Environment, and 
Agriculture Task Force; New Democrat Coalition Climate Change Task 
Force; committees: Energy and Commerce; Natural Resources; Select 
Committee on the Climate Crisis; elected to the 115th Congress on 
November 8, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
