    MIKE BOST, Republican, of Murphysboro, IL; born in Murphysboro, 
December 30, 1960; education: University of Illinois Certified 
Firefighter II Academy; professional: state representative; small 
business owner; firefighter; military: U.S. Marine Corps, 1979-82; 
religion: Christian, non-denominational; married: Tracy Stanton, 1980; 
children: Steven, Kasey, and Kaitlin; committees: Transportation and 
Infrastructure; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 114th Congress on 
November 4, 2014; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
