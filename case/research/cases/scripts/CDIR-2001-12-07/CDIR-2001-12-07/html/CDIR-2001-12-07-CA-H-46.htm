    LORETTA SANCHEZ, Democrat, of Anaheim, CA; born in Lynwood, CA, on 
January 7, 1960; education: graduate of Chapman University; M.B.A., 
American University; specializes in assisting public agencies with 
finance matters; member, Blue Dog Coalition; Law Enforcement Caucus; 
Congressional Women's, and Hispanic Caucuses; married: Steven Brixey; 
committees: Armed Services; Education and the Workforce; elected to the 
105th Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
