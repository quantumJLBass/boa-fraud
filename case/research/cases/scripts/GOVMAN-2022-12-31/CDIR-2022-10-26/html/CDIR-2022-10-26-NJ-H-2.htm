    JEFFERSON VAN DREW, Republican, of Dennis Township, NJ; born in New 
York City, NY, February 23, 1953; education: B.S., Rutgers University, 
New Brunswick, NJ; D.D.S., Fairleigh Dickinson University, Teaneck, NJ; 
professional: retired dentist; Dennis Township Fire Commissioner, 1983-
86; Cape May County Board of Freeholders, 1994-97; Mayor, Dennis 
Township, 1994-95 and 1997-2003; member, New Jersey General Assembly, 
2002-07; Assistant Majority Leader, 2006-07; New Jersey State Senator, 
2008-19; committees: Homeland Security; Transportation and 
Infrastructure; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
