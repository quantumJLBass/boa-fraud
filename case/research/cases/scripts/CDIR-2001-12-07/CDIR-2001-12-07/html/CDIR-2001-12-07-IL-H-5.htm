    ROD R. BLAGOJEVICH, Democrat, of Chicago, IL; born in Chicago, 
December 10, 1956; B.A., Northwestern University, 1979; J.D., Pepperdine 
University Law School; admitted to the Illinois bar in 1984 and began 
community law practice in Chicago; Assistant Cook County State's 
Attorney, 1986-88; Illinois House of Representatives, 1992-96; married 
Patricia Blagojevich in 1990; one child, Amy; elected to the 105th 
Congress, November 5, 1996; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
