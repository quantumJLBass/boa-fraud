    YOUNG KIM, Republican, of La Habra, CA; born in Incheon, South 
Korea, October 18, 1962; education: B.B.A., University of Southern 
California, Los Angeles, CA, 1985; professional: business owner; 
financial analyst; staff, U.S. Representative Ed Royce of California, 
1993-2012; member, California state assembly, 2014-16; religion: 
Christian; married: Charles Kim; children: Alvin Kim, Kelly Kim, 
Christine Kim, and Hannah Kim; caucuses: co-chair, SALT Caucus; Armenia 
Caucus; Global Investment in American Jobs Caucus; India Caucus; 
International Conservation Caucus; Pacific Islands Caucus; Problem 
Solvers Caucus; Taiwan Caucus; Victims of Communism Caucus; committees: 
Foreign Affairs; Science, Space, and Technology; Small Business; elected 
to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
