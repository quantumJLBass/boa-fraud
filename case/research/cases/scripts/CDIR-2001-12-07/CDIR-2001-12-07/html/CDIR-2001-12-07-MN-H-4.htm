    BETTY McCOLLUM, Democrat, of North St. Paul, MN; born on July 12, 
1954, in Minneapolis, MN; education: A.A., Inver Hills Community 
College; B.S., College of St. Catherine; employment: teacher and sales 
manager; married: Doug McCollum; children: Sean and Katie; public 
service: North St. Paul City Council, 1986-1992; Minnesota House of 
Representatives, 1992-2000; organizations: National Conference of State 
Legislatures; Mississippi Parkway Commission; Girl Scouts of America; 
Minnesota Board of Medical Practice; VFW Ladies' Auxiliary; and American 
Legion Ladies' Auxiliary; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 
2000.
