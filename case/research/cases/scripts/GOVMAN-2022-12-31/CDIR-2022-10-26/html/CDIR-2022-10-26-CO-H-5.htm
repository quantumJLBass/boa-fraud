    DOUG LAMBORN, Republican, of Colorado Springs, CO; born in 
Leavenworth, KS, May 24, 1954; education: B.S., University of Kansas, 
Lawrence, 1978; J.D., University of Kansas, Lawrence, 1985; 
professional: lawyer, private practice (business and real estate); 
Colorado State House of Representatives, 1995-98; Colorado State Senate, 
1998-2006; married: Jeanie; children: five; committees: Armed Services; 
Natural Resources; elected to the 110th Congress on November 7, 2006; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
