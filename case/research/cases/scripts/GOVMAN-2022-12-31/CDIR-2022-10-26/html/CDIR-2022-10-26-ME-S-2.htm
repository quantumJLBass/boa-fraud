    ANGUS S. KING, Jr., Independent, of Brunswick, ME; born in 
Alexandria, VA, March 31, 1944; education: graduated, Dartmouth College, 
1966; J.D., University of Virginia Law School, 1969; professional: Chief 
Counsel to U.S. Senate Subcommittee on Alcoholism and Narcotics for 
former Maine Senator William Hathaway; founded Northeast Energy 
Management, Inc., 1989; elected Maine's 72nd Governor, 1994; reelected 
1998 by one of the largest margins in Maine's history; Maine's first 
Independent U.S. Senator; committees: Armed Services; Energy and Natural 
Resources; Rules and Administration; Joint Committee on Printing; 
Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence; elected to the U.S. Senate 
on November 6, 2012; reelected to the U.S. Senate on November 6, 2018.
