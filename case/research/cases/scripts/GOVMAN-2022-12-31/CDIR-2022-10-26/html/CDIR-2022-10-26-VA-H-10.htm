    JENNIFER WEXTON, Democrat, of Leesburg, VA; born in Washington, DC, 
May 27, 1968; education: B.A., University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 
1992; J.D., College of William and Mary Law School, Williamsburg, VA, 
1995; professional: lawyer, private practice; Assistant Commonwealth's 
Attorney, Loudoun County, VA, 2001-05; substitute judge, Loudoun, VA, 
2010-11; member of the Virginia State Senate, 2014-18; married: Andrew; 
children: Jamie and Matthew; caucuses: Animal Protection Caucus; 
Chesapeake Bay Task Force; Freshman Working Group on Addiction; LGBT 
Equality Caucus; Pro-Choice Caucus; SEEC Caucus; Small Brewers Caucus; 
Transgender Equality Task Force; committees: Appropriations; Budget; 
elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 
117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
