    JAY OBERNOLTE, Republican, of Big Bear Lake, CA; born in Chicago, 
IL, August 18, 1970; education: B.S., California Institute of 
Technology, Pasadena, CA, 1992; M.S., University of California, Los 
Angeles, CA, 1997; professional: entrepreneur; pilot; member: Big Bear 
airport board of directors, 2005-10; Big Bear city council, 2010-14; 
Mayor, Big Bear, CA; California State Assemblyman, 2014-20; committees: 
Budget; Natural Resources; Science, Space, and Technology; elected to 
the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
