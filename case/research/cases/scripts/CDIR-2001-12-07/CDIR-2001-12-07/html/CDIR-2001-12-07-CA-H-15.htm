    MIKE HONDA, Democrat, of San Jose, CA; born on June 27, 1941, in 
Walnut Creek, CA; education: San Jose State University, received degrees 
in Biological Sciences and Spanish, and a Masters Degree in Education; 
awards: California Federation of Teachers Legislator of the Year; 
Outreach Paratransit Services Humanitarian Award; AEA Legislator of the 
Year; Service Employees International Union Home Care Champion Award; 
Asian Law Alliance Community Impact Award; AFL-CIO Distinguished Friend 
of Labor Award; public service: Peace Corps; San Jose Planning 
Commission; San Jose Unified School Board; Santa Clara County Board of 
Supervisors; California State Assemblyman; married: Jeanne; children: 
Mark and Michelle; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
