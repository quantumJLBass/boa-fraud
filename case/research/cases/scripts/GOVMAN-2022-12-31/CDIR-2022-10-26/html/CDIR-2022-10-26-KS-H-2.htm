    JAKE LaTURNER, Republican, of Topeka, KS; born in Galena, KS, 
February 17, 1988; education: B.A., Pittsburg State University, 
Pittsburg, KS; professional: customer service representative; staff,

2013-17; Kansas state treasurer, 2017-20; committees: Homeland Security; 
Oversight and Reform; Science, Space, and Technology; elected to the 
117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
