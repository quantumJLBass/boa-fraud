    VICENTE GONZALEZ, Democrat, of McAllen, TX; born in Corpus Christi, 
TX, September 4, 1967; education: G.E.D., 1985; associate degree in 
banking and finance, Del Mar College, Corpus Christi, TX, 1990; 
bachelor's degree in aviation business administration, Embry Riddle 
University, Corpus Christi, TX, 1992; J.D., Texas Wesleyan University 
School of Law (now Texas A&M School of Law), Fort Worth, TX, 1996; 
professional: lawyer; private practice, V. Gonzalez & Associates, PC, 
1997-present; religion: Roman Catholic; married: Lorena Saenz Gonzalez; 
committees: Financial Services; Foreign Affairs; elected to the 115th 
Congress on November 8, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
