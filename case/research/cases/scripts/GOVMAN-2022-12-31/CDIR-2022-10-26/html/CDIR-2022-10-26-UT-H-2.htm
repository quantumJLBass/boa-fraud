    CHRIS STEWART, Republican, of Farmington, UT; born in Logan, UT, 
July 15, 1960; education: B.S., economics, Utah State University, Logan, 
UT; professional: president and CEO, Shipley Group; independent author; 
military: pilot, U.S. Air Force; religion: Church of Jesus Christ of 
Latter-Day Saints; married: Evie; children: Sean, Dane, Lance, Kayla, 
Bryce, and Megan; committees: Appropriations; Permanent Select Committee 
on Intelligence; elected to the 113th Congress on November 6, 2012; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
