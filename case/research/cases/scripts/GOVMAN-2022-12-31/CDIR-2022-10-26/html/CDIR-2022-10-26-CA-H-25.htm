    MIKE GARCIA, Republican, of Santa Clarita, CA; born in Granada 
Hills, CA, April 24, 1976; education: graduated from Saugus High School, 
Santa Clarita, CA, 1994; B.S., political science, U.S. Naval Academy, 
Annapolis, MD, 1998; M.A., National Security Studies, Georgetown 
University, Washington, DC, 1998; professional: business executive, 
Raytheon Company, 2009-18; real estate developer; military: U.S. Navy, 
1999-2009; U.S. Navy Reserve, 2009-12; married: Rebecca Garcia; two 
children; committees: Appropriations; Science, Space, and Technology; 
elected to the 116th Congress by special election on May 12, 2020, to 
fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of U.S. Representative Katie 
Hill; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
