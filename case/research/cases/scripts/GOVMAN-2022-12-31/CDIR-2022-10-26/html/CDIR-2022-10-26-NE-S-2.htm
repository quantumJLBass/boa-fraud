    BEN SASSE, Republican, of Fremont, NE; born in Plainview, NE, 
February 22, 1972; education: B.A., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 
1994; M.A., St. John's College, Annapolis, MD, 1998; M.A., M.Phil., and 
Ph.D., Yale University, New Haven, CT, 2004; professional: management 
strategist; policy strategist; historian; college president; religion: 
Presbyterian/Evangelical; married: Melissa McLeod Sasse; three children: 
Elizabeth, Alexandra, and Breck; committees: Budget; Finance; Judiciary; 
Select Committee on Intelligence; elected to the U.S. Senate on November 
4, 2014; reelected to the U.S. Senate on November 3, 2020.
