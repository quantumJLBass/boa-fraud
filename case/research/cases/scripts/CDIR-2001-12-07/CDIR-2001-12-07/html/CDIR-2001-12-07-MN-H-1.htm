    GILBERT W. GUTKNECHT, Republican, of Rochester, MN; born in Cedar 
Falls, IA, on March 20, 1951; education: graduated, Cedar Falls High 
School; B.A., University of Northern Iowa; employment: real estate 
broker and auctioneer; served as State Representative, 1982-94, floor 
leader, House Republican Caucus; married: Mary Catherine Gutknecht, 
1972; children: Margie, Paul, and Emily; elected to the 104th Congress; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
