    LOUIE GOHMERT, Republican, of Tyler, TX; born in Pittsburg, TX, 
August 18, 1953; education: B.A., Texas A&M University, College Station, 
TX, 1975; J.D., Baylor University, Waco,

[[Page 254]]

TX, 1977; professional: U.S. Army, 1978-82; District Judge, Smith 
County, 1992-2002; appointed by Governor Rick Perry to complete an 
unexpired term as Chief Justice of the 12th Court of Appeals, 2002-03; 
Brigade Commander of the Corps of Cadets, Texas A&M University; 
organizations: president of the South Tyler Rotary Club; Boy Scout 
District Board of Directors; religion: deacon of Green Acres Baptist 
Church; director of Leadership Tyler; director of Centrepoint 
Ministries; married: Kathy; children: Katy, Caroline, and Sarah; 
committees: Judiciary; Natural Resources; elected to the 109th Congress 
on November 2, 2004; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
