    PETE STAUBER, Republican, of Hermantown, MN; born in Duluth, MN, 
May 10, 1966; education: B.S., Lake Superior State University, Sault 
Sainte Marie, MI, 1990; professional: professional hockey player, 
Detroit Red Wings organization, National Hockey League, 1990-93; police 
officer, Duluth, MN Police Department, 1995-2017; member, Hermantown, MN 
Town Council, 2001-05 and 2011-13; member, St. Louis County Board of 
Commissioners, Duluth, MN, 2013-19; committees: Natural Resources; Small 
Business; Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 116th 
Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
