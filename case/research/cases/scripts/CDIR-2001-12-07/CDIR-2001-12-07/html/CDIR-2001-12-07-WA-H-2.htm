    RICK LARSEN, Democrat, of Lake Stevens, WA; born on June 15, 1965, 
in Arlington, WA; education: B.A., Pacific Lutheran University; M.P.A., 
University of Minnesota; employment: economic development official at 
the Port of Everett; worked as a Director of Public Affairs for a health 
provider association; public service: Snohomish County Council; 
religion: Methodist; married: Tiia Karlen; children: Robert and Per; 
elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
