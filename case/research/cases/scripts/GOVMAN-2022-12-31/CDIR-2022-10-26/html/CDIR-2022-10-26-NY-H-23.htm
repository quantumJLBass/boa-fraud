    JOSEPH SEMPOLINSKI, Republican, of Canisteo, NY; born in Elmira, 
NY, February 10, 1983; education: B.A., Georgetown University, 
Washington, DC, 2005; professional: staff, Archbishop Walsh Academy, 
Olean, NY; consultant; campaign manager; staff, United States 
Representative Thomas W. Reed II, 2010-17; staff, New York state 
representative Joseph Giglio, 2022; committees: Budget; Education and 
Labor; elected to the 117th Congress, by special election, on August 23, 
2022, to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of U.S. 
Representative Tom Reed.
