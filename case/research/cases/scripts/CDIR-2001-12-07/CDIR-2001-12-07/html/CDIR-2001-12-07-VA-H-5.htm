    VIRGIL H. GOODE, Jr., Independent, of Rocky Mount, VA; born in 
Richmond, VA, October 17, 1946; education: B.A., University of Richmond, 
1969; J.D., University of Virginia Law School, 1973; served in Virginia 
Army National Guard; admitted to the Virginia bar in 1973; attorney; 
member, Virginia State Senate, 1973-97; former member: Ruritan Chamber 
of Commerce, Jaycees; married: Lucy Dodson Goode in 1991; children: 
Catherine; elected to the 105th Congress; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
