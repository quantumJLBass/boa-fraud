    MARK R. WARNER, Democrat, of Alexandria, VA; born in Indianapolis, 
IN, December 15, 1954; son of Robert and Marge Warner of Vernon, CT; 
education: B.A., political science, George Washington University, 1977; 
J.D., Harvard Law School, 1980; professional: Governor, Commonwealth of 
Virginia, 2002-06; chairman of the National Governor's Association, 
2004-05; religion: Presbyterian; wife: Lisa Collis; children: Madison, 
Gillian, and Eliza; committees: chair, Select Committee on Intelligence; 
Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs; Budget; Finance; Rules and 
Administration; Joint Committee on the Library; elected to the U.S. 
Senate on November 4, 2008; reelected to each succeeding Senate term.
