    BRIAN HIGGINS, Democrat, of Buffalo, NY; born in Buffalo, October 
6, 1959; education: B.A., Buffalo State College, NY, 1984; M.P.A., 
Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 1996; professional: lecturer, Buffalo 
State College; member of the Buffalo Common Council, 1988-93; member of 
the New York State Assembly, 1999-2004; children: John and Maeve; 
committees: Budget; Ways and Means; elected to the 109th Congress on 
November 2, 2004; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
