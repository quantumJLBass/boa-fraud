    TODD C. YOUNG, Republican, of Greenwood, IN; born in Lancaster, PA, 
August 24, 1972; education: B.S., political science, United States Naval 
Academy, Annapolis, MD, 1995; M.B.A., University of Chicago, Chicago, 
IL, 2000; M.A., American history, School of Advanced Study, University 
of London, UK, 2001; J.D., Indiana University, Bloomington, IN, 2005; 
professional: legislative assistant, U.S. Senate, 2002-03; management 
consultant, Crowe Chizek, 2003-05; attorney, Tucker and Tucker, PC, in 
Paoli, IN, 2005-09; military: U.S. Navy, 1990-95; U.S. Marine Corps, 
1995-2000; member: Sherwood Oaks Christian Church, Bloomington, IN; 
married: Jennifer Tucker Hill; children: Tucker, Annalise, Abigail, and 
Ava; committees: Commerce, Science, and Transportation; Finance; Foreign 
Relations; Small Business and Entrepreneurship; elected to the 112th 
Congress on November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress; 
elected to the U.S. Senate on November 8, 2016.
