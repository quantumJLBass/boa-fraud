    DENNIS MOORE, Democrat, of Lenexa, KS; born on November 8, 1945, in 
Anthony, KS; education: Jefferson Elementary School, and Charles Curtis 
Intermediate School, Wichita, KS; B.A., University of Kansas, 1967; 
J.D., Washburn University of Law, 1970; profession: attorney; admitted 
to Kansas Bar, 1970, first practiced in Topeka, KS; Assistant Attorney 
General of Kansas, 1971-73; Johnson County District Attorney, 1977-89; 
Johnson County Community College Board of Trustees, 1993-99; member, 
American Legion; married: Stephene Moore; seven children: Todd, Scott, 
Andrew, Felicia Barge, Valerie Rock, Nathan Hansen, and Adam Hansen; 
military: U.S. Army, 2nd Lieutenant, 1970; U.S. Army Reserves, Captain, 
1970-73; committees: Budget; Financial Services; Science; subcommittees: 
Capital Markets, Insurance and Government Sponsored Enterprises; 
Financial Institutions and Consumer Credit; Oversight and 
Investigations; Research; Space and Aeronautics; elected to the 106th 
Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
