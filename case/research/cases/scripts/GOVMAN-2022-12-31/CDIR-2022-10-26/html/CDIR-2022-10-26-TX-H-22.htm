    TROY E. NEHLS, Republican, of Richmond, TX; born in Beaver Dam, WI, 
April 7, 1968; education: B.A., Liberty University, Lynchburg, VA; M.S., 
University of Houston-Downtown, Houston, TX; professional: Wisconsin 
Army National Guard; United States Army Reserve; police officer; deputy 
constable, Fort Bend County, TX, 1998-2002; deputy sheriff, Fort Bend 
County, TX, 2002-04; constable, Fort Bend County, TX, 2005-12; sheriff, 
Fort Bend County, TX, 2013-20; committees: Transportation and 
Infrastructure; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
