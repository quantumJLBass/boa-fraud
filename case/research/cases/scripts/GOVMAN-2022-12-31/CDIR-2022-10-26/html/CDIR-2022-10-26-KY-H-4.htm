    THOMAS MASSIE, Republican, of Garrison, KY; born in Huntington, WV, 
January 13, 1971; education: graduated from Lewis County High School; 
B.S., electrical engineering / economics, Massachusetts Institute of 
Technology, Cambridge, MA, 1993; M.S., mechanical engineering, 
Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 1996; professional: inventor / 
engineer; founder of SensAble Devices, Inc.; farmer; Lewis County Judge 
Executive; married: Rhonda; four children; committees: Judiciary; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected simultaneously to the 112th 
and 113th Congresses on November 6, 2012, by special election, to fill 
the vacancy caused by the resignation of U.S. Representative Geoffrey C. 
``Geoff'' Davis; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
