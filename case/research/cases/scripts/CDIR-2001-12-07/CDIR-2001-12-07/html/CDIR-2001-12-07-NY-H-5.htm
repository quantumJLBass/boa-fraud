    GARY L. ACKERMAN, Democrat, of Queens, NY; born in Brooklyn, NY, 
November 19, 1942; education: graduate, Queens College, Flushing, NY; 
attended St. John's University, Jamaica, NY; public school teacher; 
newspaper editor; businessman; New York State Senate, 1979-83; married: 
the former Rita Tewel; children: Lauren, Corey, and Ari; elected by 
special election on March 1, 1983, to the 98th Congress, to fill the 
vacancy caused by the death of Benjamin Rosenthal; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
