    CATHERINE CORTEZ MASTO, Democrat, of Las Vegas, NV; born in Las 
Vegas, March 29, 1964; education: graduated Clark High School, Las 
Vegas; B.S., finance, University of Nevada, Reno, 1986; J.D., Gonzaga 
University, Spokane, WA, 1990; admitted to the Nevada State Bar in 1990; 
professional: Chief of Staff to Nevada Governor Bob Miller; Criminal 
Prosecutor, U.S. Attorney's Office, Washington, DC; elected Nevada 
Attorney General, 2007-15; executive vice chancellor for the Nevada 
System of Higher Education; committees: Banking, Housing, and Urban 
Affairs; Energy and Natural Resources; Finance; Indian Affairs; elected 
to the U.S. Senate on November 8, 2016.
