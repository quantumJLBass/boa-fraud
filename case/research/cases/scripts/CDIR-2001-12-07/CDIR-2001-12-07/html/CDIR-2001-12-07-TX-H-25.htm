    KEN BENTSEN, Democrat, of Houston, TX; born in Houston, June 3, 
1959; education: graduated, Deerfield Academy, 1977; B.A., University of 
St. Thomas, Houston, 1982; M.P.A., American University, Washington, DC, 
1985; legislative assistant to Ronald D. Coleman, 1983-87; served as 
associate staff to the U.S. House Committee on Appropriations; 
investment banker, 1987-94; chair, Harris County Democratic Party, 1990-
1993; serves on Financial Services and Budget Committees; religion: 
member of the First Presbyterian Church of Houston; married: the former 
Tamra Kiehn, 1990; children: Louise and Meredith; elected to the 104th 
Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
