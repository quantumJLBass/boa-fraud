    JAMAAL BOWMAN, Democrat, of Yonkers, NY; born in Manhattan, NY, 
April 1, 1976; education: B.A., University of New Haven, West Haven, CT, 
1999; M.A., Mercy College, Dobbs Ferry, NY, 2006; Ed.D., Manhattanville 
College, Harrison, NY, 2019; professional: educator and counselor in the 
New York City public school system, 1999-2019; founding principal of 
Cornerstone Academy for Social Action Middle School, 2009-19; married: 
Melissa Oppenheimer; three children; caucuses: Addiction, Treatment, and 
Recovery Caucus; Afterschool Caucus; Black Jewish Caucus; Black Maternal 
Health Caucus; Caribbean Caucus; Congressional Antitrust Caucus; 
Congressional Asthma and Allergy Caucus; Congressional Black Caucus; 
Congressional Career and Technical Education Caucus; Congressional 
Progressive Caucus; Foster Youth Caucus; LGBTQ+ Equality Caucus; Mental 
Health Caucus; Pro-Choice Caucus; committees: vice chair, Education and 
Labor; Science, Space, and Technology; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
