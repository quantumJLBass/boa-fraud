    TONY GONZALES, Republican, of San Antonio, TX; born in Anniston, 
AL, October 10, 1980; education: A.A., Chaminade University, Honolulu, 
HI, 2004; B.S., Excelsior College, Albany, NY, 2007; M.A., American 
Public University, Charles Town, WV, 2014; Graduate degree in 
legislative studies, Georgetown University, Washington, DC, 2018; 
professional: United States Navy, 1999-2019; faculty, Pensacola State 
College, Pensacola, FL; faculty, University of Maryland--Global Campus, 
College Park, MD; community representative, Head Start policy council, 
San Antonio, TX, 2018-19; legislative fellow, Office of Legislative 
Affairs, United States Navy, 2016-18; religion: Catholic; committees: 
Appropriations; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
