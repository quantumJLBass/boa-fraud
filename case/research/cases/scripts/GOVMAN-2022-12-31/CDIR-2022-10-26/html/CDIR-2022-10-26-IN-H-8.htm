    LARRY BUCSHON, Republican, of Evansville, IN; born in Kincaid, IL, 
May 31, 1962; education: graduated from South Fork High School, Kincaid, 
IL, 1980; B.S., with a concentration in chemistry, University of 
Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, IL, 1984; M.D., University of Illinois, 
Chicago, 1988; professional: residency, Medical College of Wisconsin in 
Milwaukee, 1988-95; cardiothoracic surgeon, 1995-2010; commissioned 
lieutenant, U.S. Navy Reserve, 1989; promoted, lieutenant commander, 
1994; honorable discharge, 1998; married: Kathryn; children: Luke, Alec, 
Blair, and Zoe; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected to the 112th 
Congress on November 2, 2010; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
