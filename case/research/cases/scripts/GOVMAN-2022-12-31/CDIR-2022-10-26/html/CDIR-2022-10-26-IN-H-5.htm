    VICTORIA SPARTZ, Republican, of Noblesville, IN; born in Nosivka, 
Chernihiv Oblast, Ukraine, October 6, 1978; education: B.S. and M.B.A.,

Education and Labor; Judiciary; elected to the 117th Congress on
