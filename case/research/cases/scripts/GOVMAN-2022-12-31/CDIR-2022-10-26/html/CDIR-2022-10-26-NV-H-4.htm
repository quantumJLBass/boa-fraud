    STEVEN HORSFORD, Democrat, of North Las Vegas, NV; born in Las 
Vegas, April 29, 1973; education: University of Nevada, Reno, NV; 
professional: CEO, Culinary Training Academy; member of the Nevada State 
Senate, 2004-12; majority leader, Nevada State Senate, 2009-12; 
religion: Baptist; married: Dr. Sonya Horsford; caucuses: Black Maternal 
Health Caucus; Congressional Asian Pacific American Caucus; 
Congressional Black Caucus; Congressional Progressive Caucus; Gun 
Violence Prevention Task Force; Labor Caucus; LGTBQ+ Equality Caucus; 
New Democrats Coalition; Problem Solvers Caucus; Pro-Choice Caucus; 
committees: Armed Services; Budget; Ways and Means; elected to the 113th 
Congress on November 6, 2012; elected to the 116th Congress on November 
6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
