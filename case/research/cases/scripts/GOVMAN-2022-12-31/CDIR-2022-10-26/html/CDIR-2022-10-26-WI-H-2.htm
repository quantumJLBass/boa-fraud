    MARK POCAN, Democrat, of the Town of Vermont, WI; born in Kenosha, 
WI, August 14, 1964; education: graduated from Bradford High School, 
1982; journalism, University of Wisconsin, 1986; professional: small 
business owner, 1986-present; elected to the Dane County Board of 
Supervisors, 1991-96; elected to the State Assembly from the 78th 
District, 1999-2013; married: Philip Frank in 2006; committees: 
Appropriations; Education and Labor; elected to the 113th Congress on 
November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
