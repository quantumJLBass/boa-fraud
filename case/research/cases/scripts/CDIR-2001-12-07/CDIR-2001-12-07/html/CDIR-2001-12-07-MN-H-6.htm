    BILL LUTHER, Democrat, of Stillwater, MN; born in Fergus Falls, MN, 
on June 27, 1945; education: graduated, Fergus Falls High School, 1963; 
B.S., University of Minnesota, 1967; J.D., University of Minnesota, 
1970; admitted to the State bar in 1970; member: Minnesota State House, 
1975-76; Minnesota State Senate, 1977-94; Senate assistant majority 
leader, 1983-94; religion: member, St. Alphonsus Catholic Church; 
married: Darlene Luther; children: Alexander and Alicia; committee: 
Energy and Commerce; elected to the 104th Congress; reelected to each 
succeeding Congress.
