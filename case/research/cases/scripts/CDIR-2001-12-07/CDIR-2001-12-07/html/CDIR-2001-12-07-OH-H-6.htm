    TED STRICKLAND, Democrat, of Lucasville, OH: born in Lucasville, 
August 4, 1941; education: B.A., history, Asbury College, 1963; M. Div., 
Asbury College, 1967; M.A., 1967; M.A., Ph.D. (1980), counseling 
psychology, University of Kentucky; employment: psychologist and 
educator: director of a Methodist children's home, professor at Shawnee 
State University, and consulting psychologist at Southern Ohio 
Correctional Facility; married: Francis Smith Strickland; elected to the 
103rd and 105th Congresses; reelected to each succeeding Congress; 
committee: Energy and Commerce.
