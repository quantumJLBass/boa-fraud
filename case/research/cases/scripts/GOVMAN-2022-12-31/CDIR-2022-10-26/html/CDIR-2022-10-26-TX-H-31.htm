    JOHN R. CARTER, Republican, of Round Rock, TX; born in Houston, TX, 
November 6, 1941; education: Texas Tech University, Lubbock, TX, 1964; 
University of Texas Law School, 1969; professional: attorney; private 
law practice; public service: appointed and elected a Texas District 
Court Judge, 1981-2001; awards: recipient and namesake of the Williamson 
County ``John R. Carter Lifetime Achievement Award''; family: married to 
Erika Carter; children: Gilianne, John, Theodore, and Danielle; 
committees: Appropriations; elected to the 108th Congress on November 5, 
2002; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
