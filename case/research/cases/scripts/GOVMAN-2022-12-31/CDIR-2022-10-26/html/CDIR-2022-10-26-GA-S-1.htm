    JON OSSOFF, Democrat, of Atlanta, GA; born in Atlanta, February 16, 
1987; education: B.S., Georgetown University School of Foreign Service, 
Washington, DC, 2009; M.Sc., London School of Economics, London, United 
Kingdom, 2013; professional: national security aide to Georgia 
Representative Hank Johnson, 2007-12; investigative journalist, producer 
of investigations around official corruption, organized crime, and war 
crimes, CEO of Insight TWI 2013-21; religion: Jewish; family: wife, Dr. 
Alisha Kramer, M.D., M.Sc.; committees: Banking, Housing, and Urban 
Affairs; Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs; Judiciary; Rules 
and Administration; elected to the U.S. Senate in the January 5, 2021 
general election runoff for the term ending January 3, 2027; took the 
oath of office on January 20, 2021.
