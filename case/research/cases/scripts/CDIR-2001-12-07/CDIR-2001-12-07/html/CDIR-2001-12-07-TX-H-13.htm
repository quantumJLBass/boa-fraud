    WILLIAM (MAC) THORNBERRY, Republican, of Clarendon, TX; born in 
Clarendon, TX, July 15, 1958; education: valedictorian graduate of 
Clarendon High School; B.A., Texas Tech University; University of Texas, 
law degree; employment: rancher, attorney; admitted to the Texas bar, 
1983; member: Texas and Southwestern Cattle Raisers; co-chair of the 
Congressional Oil and Gas Forum; Rural Health Care Coalition, and 
chairman of the Armed Services Special Oversight Panel on the Department 
of Energy Reorganization; married: Sarah Adams, 1986; children: Will and 
Mary Kemp; elected to the 104th Congress; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
