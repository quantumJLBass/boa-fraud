    PATRICK T. McHENRY, Republican, of Denver, NC; born in Gastonia, 
NC, October 22, 1975; education: graduated Ashbrook High School, 
Gastonia, NC; attended North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC; 
B.A., Belmont Abbey College, Belmont, NC, 1999; professional: realtor; 
media executive; appointed special assistant to the U.S. Secretary of 
Labor by President George W. Bush in 2001; member, North Carolina House 
of Representatives, 2003-05; organizations: Gastonia Rotary Club; Holy 
Spirit Catholic Church, board of directors; the National Rifle 
Association; married: Giulia, 2010; children: Cecelia and Rese; served 
as Chief Deputy Whip from June 2014 to December 2018; committees: 
ranking member, Financial Services; elected to the 109th Congress on 
November 2, 2004; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
