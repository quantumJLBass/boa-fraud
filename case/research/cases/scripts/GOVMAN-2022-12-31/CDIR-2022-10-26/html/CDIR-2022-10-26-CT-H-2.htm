    JOE COURTNEY, Democrat, of Vernon, CT; born in Hartford, CT, April 
6, 1953; education: B.A., Tufts University, Middlesex County, MA, 1975; 
University of Connecticut School of Law, Hartford, CT, 1978; public 
service: Connecticut State Representative, 1987-94; Vernon Town 
Attorney, 2003-06; professional: attorney, Courtney, Boyan, and Foran, 
LLC, 1978-2006; religion: Roman Catholic; married: Audrey Courtney; 
children: Robert and Elizabeth; committees: Armed Services; Education 
and Labor; elected to the 110th Congress on November 7, 2006; reelected 
to each succeeding Congress.
