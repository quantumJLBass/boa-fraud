    KELLY ARMSTRONG, Republican, of Dickinson, ND; born in Dickinson, 
October 8, 1976; education: B.A., psychology, University of North 
Dakota, Grand Forks, ND, 2001; J.D., Univer

partner, Reichert Armstrong law firm; vice president, Armstrong 
Corporation; volunteer fireman, 2004-12; president, Dickinson Baseball

Dakota State Senator, 2013-18; married: Kjersti Armstrong; children: 
Anna and Eli; committees: Energy and Commerce; Ethics; elected to the 
116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
