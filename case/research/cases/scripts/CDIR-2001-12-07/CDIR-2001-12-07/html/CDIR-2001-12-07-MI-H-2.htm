    PETER HOEKSTRA, Republican, of Holland, MI; born in Groningen, the 
Netherlands, on October 30, 1953; education: graduated, Holland 
Christian High School; B.A., Hope College, Holland, 1975; M.B.A., 
University of Michigan, 1977; employment: vice president for product 
management, Herman Miller, Inc.; married: the former Diane Johnson; 
children: Erin, Allison, and Bryan; elected on November 3, 1992 to the 
103rd Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
