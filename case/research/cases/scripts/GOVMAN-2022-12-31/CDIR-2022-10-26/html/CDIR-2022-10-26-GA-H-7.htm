    CAROLYN BOURDEAUX, Democrat, of Suwanee, GA; born in Roanoke, VA, 
June 3, 1970; education: B.A., Yale University, New Haven, CT, 1992; 
M.P.A., University of Southern California, Los Angeles, CA, 1999; Ph.D., 
Syracuse University, Syracuse, NY, 2003; professional: staff, United 
States Representative and Senator Ron Wyden, 1993-97; professor, Georgia 
State University, Atlanta, GA, 2003-20; director, Georgia State Senate 
Budget and Evaluation Office, 2007-10; committees: Small Business; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
