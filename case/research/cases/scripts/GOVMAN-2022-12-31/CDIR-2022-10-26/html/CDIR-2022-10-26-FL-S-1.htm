    MARCO A. RUBIO, Republican, of West Miami, FL; born in Miami, May 
28, 1971; education: South Miami Senior High School, Miami, FL, 1989; 
B.S., political science, University of Florida, Gainsville, FL, 1993; 
J.D., cum laude, University of Miami, Miami-Dade County, FL, 1996; 
professional: Florida House of Representatives, 2000-08; served as 
Majority Whip, Majority Leader, and Speaker of the House; attorney, 
Broad and Cassel; Marco Rubio, P.A.; lecturer and senior fellow at 
Florida International University, 2009-present; Bob Dole for President, 
1996, Miami-Dade County Director; religion: Roman Catholic; married: 
Jeanette; children: Amanda, Daniella, Anthony, and Dominick; Commission 
on Security and Cooperation in Europe; committees: vice chair, Select 
Committee on Intelligence; Appropriations; Foreign Relations; Small 
Business and Entrepreneurship; Special Committee on Aging; elected to 
the U.S. Senate on November 2, 2010; reelected on November 8, 2016.
