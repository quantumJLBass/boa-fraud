    EDWARD L. SCHROCK, Republican, of Virginia Beach, VA; born in 
Middletown, OH, on April 6, 1941; education: B.A., Alderson-Broaddus 
College; M.A., American University; military service: U.S. Navy, 1964-
88, retired with the rank of Captain; employment: investment broker; 
organizations: Friends of the Virginia Beach Public Library; Cape Henry 
Collegiate School Parent / Faculty Association; Library of Virginia 
Foundation Board; Lee's Friends (cancer victim support organization); 
Governor's Commission on Information Technology; married: Judith; 
children: Randy; religion: Baptist; public service: Virginia State 
Senate, 1995-2000; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
