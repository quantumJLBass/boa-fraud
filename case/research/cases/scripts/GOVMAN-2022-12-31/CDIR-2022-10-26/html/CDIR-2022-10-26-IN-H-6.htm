    GREG PENCE, Republican, of Columbus, IN; born in Columbus, November 
13, 1956; education: B.A., theology and philosophy, Loyola University of 
Chicago, Chicago, IL, 1979; M.B.A., Loyola University of Chicago, 
Chicago, IL, 1985; military: U.S. Marine Corps, 1979-83; professional: 
president, Kiel Brothers Oil Company, 1998-2004; owner / operator, 
antique malls; committees: Energy and Commerce; elected to the 116th 
Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
