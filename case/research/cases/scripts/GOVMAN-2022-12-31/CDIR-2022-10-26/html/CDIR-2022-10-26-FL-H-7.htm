    STEPHANIE N. MURPHY, Democrat, of Winter Park, FL; born in Ho Chi 
Minh City, Vietnam, September 16, 1978; education: B.A., economics and 
international relations, College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, VA, 
2000; M.S., foreign service, Georgetown University, Washington, DC, 
2004; professional: strategy and operations consultant, Deloitte, 2000-
02; national security specialist, Office of the Under Secretary of 
Defense for Policy, U.S. Department of Defense, 2004-08; executive, 
SunGate Capital LLC, 2008-16; instructor, Rollins College, 2014-16; 
awards: Secretary of Defense Medal for Exceptional Civilian Service; 
married: Sean Murphy; children: Liem and Maya; appointments: U.S. 
Military Academy West Point Board of Visitors; caucuses: chair emeritus, 
Future Forum; co-chair, Blue Dog Coalition; co-chair, Modeling, 
Simulation, and Training Caucus; Climate Solutions Caucus; Congressional 
Asian Pacific American Caucus; Equality Caucus; Gun Violence Prevention 
Task Force; New Democrat Coalition; Problem Solvers Caucus; committees: 
Armed Services; Ways and Means; elected to the 115th Congress on 
November 8, 2016; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
