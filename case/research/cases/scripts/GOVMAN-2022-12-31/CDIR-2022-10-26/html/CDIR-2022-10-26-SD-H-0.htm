    DUSTY JOHNSON, Republican, of Mitchell, SD; born in Pierre, SD, 
September 30, 1976; education: B.A., University of South Dakota, 
Vermillion, SD, 1999; M.P.A., University of Kansas, Lawrence, KS, 2002; 
professional: vice president of consulting, Vantage Point Solutions, 
2014-18; chief of staff, Office of Governor Daugaard of South Dakota, 
2011-14; South Dakota Public Utilities Commissioner, 2005-11; senior 
policy advisor, Office of Governor Mike Rounds of South Dakota, 2003-05; 
family: married to Jacquelyn; children: three sons, Max,

[[Page 246]]

Benjamin, and Owen; committees: Agriculture; Transportation and 
Infrastructure; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; 
reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
