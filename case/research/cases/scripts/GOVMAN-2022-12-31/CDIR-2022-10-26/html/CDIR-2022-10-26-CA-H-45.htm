    KATIE PORTER, Democrat, of Irvine, CA; born in Fort Dodge, Webster 
County, IA, January 3, 1974; education: B.A., Yale University, New 
Haven, CT, 1996; J.D., Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, 2001; 
professional: law professor; staff, Office of the Attorney General, 
California State Department of Justice, 2012-14; faculty, University of 
California, Irvine, CA, 2011-18; children: Luke, Paul, and Betsey; 
committees: Natural Resources; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 
116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
