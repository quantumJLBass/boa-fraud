    BERNARD SANDERS, Independent, of Burlington, VT; born in Brooklyn, 
NY, on 
September 8, 1941; education: graduated from Madison High School, 
Brooklyn, B.S., political science, University of Chicago, 1964; 
employment: carpenter, writer, college professor; mayor 

[[Page 274]]

of Burlington, 
VT, 1981-89; married: the former Jane O'Meara, 1988; children: Levi, 
Heather, Carina and David; elected to the 102nd Congress on November 6, 
1990; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
