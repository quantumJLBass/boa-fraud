    PATRICK J. TIBERI, Republican, of Columbus, OH; born on October 21, 
1962, in Columbus, OH; education: B.A., Ohio State University, 1985; 
employment: real estate agent; assistant to Representative John Kasich 
(R-OH); public service: Ohio House of Representatives, 1992-2000; served 
as Majority Leader; organizations: American Red Cross Columbus Chapter 
Advisory Board; Westerville Chamber of Commerce; Columbus Board of 
Realtors; Military Veterans and Community Service Commission; Sons of 
Italy; awards: Fraternal Order of Police Outstanding Legislator; 
Watchdog of the Treasury Award; American Red Cross Volunteer Service 
Award; married: Denice; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 
2000.
