    JERRY LEWIS, Republican, of Redlands, CA; born in Seattle, WA, 
October 21, 1934; education: graduated, San Bernardino High School, 
1952; B.A., UCLA, 1956; graduate intern in public affairs, Coro 
Foundation; life underwriter; former member, San Bernardino School 
Board; served in California State Assembly, 1968-78; insurance 
executive, 1959-78; married to Arlene Willis; seven children; elected to 
the 96th Congress, November 7, 1978; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
