    CONOR LAMB, Democrat, of Mt. Lebanon, PA; born in Washington, DC, 
June 27, 1984; education: graduated from Pittsburgh Central Catholic 
High School, 2002; University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, 2006; 
J.D., University of Pennsylvania Law School, 2009; Captain, active duty 
U.S. Marine Corps, 2009-13; Assistant U.S. Attorney, Pittsburgh, PA, 
2014-17; caucuses: Congressional LGBTQ+ Equality Caucus; Expand Social 
Security Caucus; For Country Caucus; Problem Solvers Caucus; Steel 
Caucus; committees: Science, Space, and Technology; Transportation and 
Infrastructure; Veterans' Affairs; elected by special election on March 
13, 2018 to represent the 18th District in the 115th Congress; elected 
to represent the 17th District in the 116th Congress on November 6, 
2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
