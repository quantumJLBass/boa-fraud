    BARRY MOORE, Republican, of Enterprise, AL; born September 26, 
1966; education: A.S., Enterprise State Junior College, Enterprise, AL, 
1989; B.S., agricultural science, Auburn University, Auburn, AL, 1992; 
professional: founder and operator, Barry Moore Industries; Member, 
Alabama State House of Representatives, 2010-18; family: wife, Heather; 
children: Jeremy, Kathleen, Claudia, and Jeb; caucuses: Congressional 
Air Force Caucus; Congressional Army Caucus; Congressional Chicken 
Caucus; Congressional Rural Broadband Caucus; F-35 Caucus; Freedom 
Caucus; National Guard and Reserve Caucus; Republican Israel Caucus; 
committees: Agriculture; Veterans' Affairs; elected to the 117th 
Congress on November 3, 2020.
