    C. SCOTT FRANKLIN, Republican, of Lakeland, FL; born in Thomaston, 
GA, August 23, 1964; education: B.S., United States Naval Academy, 
Annapolis, MD, 1986; M.B.A., Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, 
Daytona Beach, FL, 1994; professional: United States Navy, 1986-2000; 
United States Navy Reserve, 2000-12; insurance company owner; 
commissioner, Lakeland, FL, 2018-21; Mayor pro tem, Lakeland, FL, 2020; 
committees: Armed Services; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 117th 
Congress on November 3, 2020.
