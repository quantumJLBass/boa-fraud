    JOHN M. SHIMKUS, Republican, of Collinsville, IL; born on February 
21, 1958, in Collinsville; graduated from Collinsville High School; 
B.S., West Point Military Academy, West Point, NY, 1980; teaching 
certificate, Christ College, Irvine, CA, 1990; MBA, Southern Illinois 
University, Edwardsville, 1997; U.S. Army Reserves, 1980-85; government 
and history teacher, Collinsville High School; Collinsville township 
trustee, 1989; Madison county treasurer, 1990-96; married the former 
Karen Muth, 1987; children: David, Daniel, and Joshua; elected to the 
105th Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
