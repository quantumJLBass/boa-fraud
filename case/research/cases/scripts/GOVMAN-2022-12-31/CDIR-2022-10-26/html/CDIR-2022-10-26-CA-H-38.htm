    LINDA T. SANCHEZ, Democrat, of Whittier, CA; born in Orange, CA, 
January 28, 1969; education: B.A., University of California, Berkeley; 
J.D., UCLA Law School; passed bar exam in 1995; professional: attorney; 
has practiced in the areas of appellate, civil rights, and employment 
law; International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers Local 441; National 
Electrical Contractors Association; Orange County Central Labor Council 
Executive Secretary, AFL-CIO; religion: Catholic; caucuses: co-founder 
and co-chair, Congressional Labor and Working Families Caucus; vice 
chair, LGBT Equality Caucus; Congressional Hispanic Caucus; 
Congressional Progressive Caucus; committees: Ways and Means; elected to 
the 108th Congress on November 5, 2002; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
