    DOUGLAS A. OSE, Republican, of Sacramento, CA; born in Sacramento, 
CA, on June 27, 1955; education: Rio Americano High School; B.S., 
Business Administration, University of California at Berkeley; religion: 
Lutheran; organizations: former member, Citrus Heights Incorporation 
Project Board of Directors; former member, California State Automobile 
Association Board of Directors; former member, Citrus Heights Chamber of 
Commerce; former member, Sacramento Housing and Redevelopment 
Commission; member, Sacramento Rotary Club; married: Lynnda Ose, 1989; 
children: Erika and Emily; committees: Agriculture; Financial Services; 
Government Reform; subcommittees: Capital Markets, Insurance, and 
Government Sponsored Enterprises; Conservation, Credit, and Rural 
Development and Research; Criminal Justice, Drug Policy and Human 
Resources; Domestic Monetary Policy, Technology, and Economic Growth; 
chairman, Energy Policy, Natural Resources and Regulatory Affairs; 
chairman, Environment, Resources, and Agriculture; General Farm 
Commodities and Risk Management; Government Efficiency, Financial 
Management and Intergovernmental Relations; vice chairman, International 
Monetary Policy and Trade; Technology and Procurement Policy; Retirement 
Security, Capital Markets, and Tax Policy; elected to the 106th 
Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
