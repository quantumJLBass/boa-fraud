    LUIS V. GUTIERREZ, Democrat, of Chicago, IL; born on December 10, 
1953, in Chicago; B.A., Northeastern Illinois University, 1974; 
alderman; social worker, State of Illinois; teacher; married: 1977 to 
Soraida Arocho Gutierrez; children: Omaira and Jessica; elected on 
November 3, 1992 to the 103rd Congress; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
