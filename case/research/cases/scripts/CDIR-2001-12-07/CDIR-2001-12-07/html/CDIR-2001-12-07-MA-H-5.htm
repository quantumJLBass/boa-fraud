    MARTIN T. MEEHAN, Democrat, of Lowell, MA; born in Lowell, December 
30, 1956; graduated from Lowell High School, 1974; B.A., University of 
Lowell, 1978; M.P.A., Suffolk University, Boston, MA, 1981; J.D., 
Suffolk University Law School, 1986; attorney; admitted to the 
Massachusetts bar, 1986; First Assistant District Attorney for Middlesex 
County; Deputy Secretary of State; married Ellen Murphy, July 1996; 
committees: Armed Services; Judiciary; elected on November 3, 1992 to 
the 103rd Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
