    BRIAN BAIRD, Democrat, of Vancouver, WA; born March 7, 1956 in 
Chauma, NM; B.S., University of Utah, 1977; M.S., University of Wyoming, 
1980; Ph.D., University of Wyoming, 1984; profession: licensed clincial 
psychologist; has practiced in Washington State and Oregon; Professor 
and former Chairman of the Department of Psychology at Pacific Lutheran 
University; has worked in a variety of medical environments prior to 
election to the U.S. Congress; elected President of the Democratic 
Freshman Class for the 106th Congress; Democratic Regional Whip; 
committees: Science; Small Business; Transportation and Infrastructure; 
elected to the 106th Congress; reelected to the 107th Congress.
