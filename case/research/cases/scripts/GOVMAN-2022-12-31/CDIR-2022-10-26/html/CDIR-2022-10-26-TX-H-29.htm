    SYLVIA R. GARCIA, Democrat, of Houston, TX; born in Palito Blanco, 
Jim Wells County, TX, September 6, 1950; education: graduated from Ben 
Bolt-Palito Blanco High School, Palito Blanco, TX; B.S.W., Texas Women's 
University, Denton, TX, 1972; J.D., Texas Southern University, Houston, 
TX, 1978; professional: lawyer; social worker; director and presiding 
judge, municipal court system, Houston, TX, 1983-98; city controller, 
Houston, TX, 1998-2003; member of the commissioners court, Harris 
County, TX, 2003-10; member of the Texas State Senate, 2013-19; 
religion: Catholic; committees: Armed Services; Financial Services; 
Judiciary; elected to the 116th Congress on November 6, 2018; reelected 
to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
