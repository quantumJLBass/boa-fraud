    KIRSTEN E. GILLIBRAND, Democrat, of Brunswick, NY; born in Albany, 
NY, December 9, 1966; education: B.A., Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH, 
1988; J.D., UCLA, Los Angeles, CA, 1991; professional: attorney; Special 
Counsel to the U.S. Secretary of Housing and Urban Development Andrew 
Cuomo; private legal practice; religion: Catholic; married: Jonathan 
Gillibrand, 2001; two sons: Theodore and Henry; committees: Agriculture, 
Nutrition, and Forestry; Armed Services; Select Committee on 
Intelligence; Special Committee on Aging; appointed to the 111th 
Congress on January 23, 2009, to fill the vacancy caused by the 
resignation of U.S. Senator Hillary Clinton; subsequently elected on 
November 2, 2010, for the remaining two years of the unexpired term; 
reelected to each succeeding Senate term.
