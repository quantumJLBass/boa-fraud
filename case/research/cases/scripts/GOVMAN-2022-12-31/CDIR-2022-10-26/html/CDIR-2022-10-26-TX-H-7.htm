    LIZZIE PANNILL FLETCHER, Democrat, of Harris County, TX; born in 
Houston, TX, February 13, 1975; education: graduated from St. John's 
School, Houston, TX, 1993; B.A., Kenyon College, Gambier, OH; 1997; 
J.D., College of William & Mary, Williamsburg, VA, 2006; professional: 
lawyer; married: Scott Fletcher, 2010; committees: Energy and Commerce; 
Science, Space, and Technology; elected to the 116th Congress on 
November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
