    GORDON HAROLD SMITH, Republican, of Pendleton, OR; born May 25, 
1952, in Pendleton, B.A., 1976, Brigham Young University; LL.B, 1979, 
Southwestern University; served as law clerk to Justice H. Vernon Payne 
of the New Mexico Supreme Court and practiced law in Arizona; elected to 
State of Oregon Senate, 1993; elected Oregon Senate President, 1994; 
president / owner of Smith Frozen Foods, Inc., since 1981; committees: 
Budget; Energy and Natural Resources; Foreign Relations; Commerce, 
Science, and Transportation; married: Sharon Lankford Smith, 1975; three 
children: Brittany, Garrett, and Morgan; elected to the U.S. Senate on 
November 5, 1996.
