    SHONTEL M. BROWN, Democrat, of Warrensville Heights, OH; born in 
Cleveland, OH, June 24, 1975; A.S., Cuyahoga Community College, 
Cleveland, OH, 2012; professional: Marketing Support Representative, 
Northeast Ohio, Telco Communications Group, 1997-99; Director of Sales, 
Hotcards.com, Chagrin Falls, OH, 2004-09; founder and owner, Diversified 
Digital Solutions, Cleveland, OH; Cuyahoga County, Ohio Council 
Representative, District 9, Cleveland, OH, 2015-21; committees: 
Agriculture; Oversight and Reform; elected to the 117th Congress, by 
special election, on November 2, 2021, to fill the vacancy caused by the 
resignation of U.S. Representative Marcia Fudge on March 10, 2021.
