    MICHELLE STEEL, Republican, of Orange County, CA; born in Seoul, 
South Korea, June 21, 1955; education: B.B.S., Pepperdine University, 
Malibu, CA; M.B.A., University of Southern California, Los Angeles, CA, 
2010; professional: business professional; member, California State 
Board of Equalization, 2007-15; member, 2015-17, chair, 2017-20, Orange 
County, CA Board of Supervisors; married: Shawn Steel; children: two; 
caucuses: Armenian Caucus; Congressional-Executive Committee on China; 
Congressional Study Group on Korea; House Republican Israel Caucus; 
Mental Health Caucus; Quiet Skies Caucus; SALT Caucus; Spent Nuclear 
Fuel Solutions Caucus; Taiwan Caucus; committees: Education and Labor; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
