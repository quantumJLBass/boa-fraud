    LANE EVANS, Democrat, of Rock Island, IL; born in Rock Island on 
August 4, 1951; graduated, Alleman High School, Rock Island, 1969; B.A., 
Augustana College, Rock Island, 1974; J.D., Georgetown University Law 
Center, Washington, DC, 1978; admitted to Illinois bar in 1978 and 
commenced practice in Rock Island; served in U.S. Marine Corps, 1969-71; 
attorney for the Western Illinois Legal Foundation, 1978-79; national 
staff, Kennedy for President campaign, 1980; entered private practice as 
a partner in Community Legal Clinic, 1982; legal representative for 
ACLU, APRI, and LULAC, 1979; awards: Vietnam Veterans of America 
``National Legislator of the Year'' (1985), President's Award for 
Outstanding Achievement, 1990; elected on November 2, 1982 to the 98th 
Congress; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
