    MARK TAKANO, Democrat, of Riverside, CA; born in Riverside, 
December 10, 1960; education: B.A., government, Harvard College, 
Cambridge, MA, 1983; M.A., fine arts, University of California 
Riverside, Riverside, CA, 2010; professional: public school teacher; 
Riverside Community College District Board Trustee; awards: chairman of 
the Asian Pacific Islander Caucus of the California Democratic Party; 
charter member of the Association of Latino Community College Trustees; 
member of the Association of California Asian American Trustees; member 
of Asian Pacific Americans in Higher Education; recipient of Martin 
Luther King Visionaries Award; religion: Methodist; committees: chair, 
Veterans' Affairs; Education and Labor; elected to the 113th Congress on 
November 6, 2012; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
