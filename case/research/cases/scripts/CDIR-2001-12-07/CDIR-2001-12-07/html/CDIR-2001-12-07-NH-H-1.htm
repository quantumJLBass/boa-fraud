    JOHN E. SUNUNU, Republican, of Bedford, NH; born on September 10, 
1964 in Boston, MA; graduated from Salem High School, Salem, MA, 1982; 
B.S., mechanical engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 
1986; M.S., mechanical engineering, Massachusetts Institute of 
Technology, 1987; M.B.A., Harvard University Business School, Boston, 
MA, 1991; Chief Financial Officer and Director of Operations, Teletrol 
Systems, Inc.; married Catherine Holloran Sununu, 1988; two children: 
John Hayes and Grace; committees: Appropriations; vice chair, Budget; 
subcommittees: Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related 
Programs; Treasury, Postal Service, and General Government; Veterans 
Affairs, HUD, and Independent Agencies; elected to the 105th Congress; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
