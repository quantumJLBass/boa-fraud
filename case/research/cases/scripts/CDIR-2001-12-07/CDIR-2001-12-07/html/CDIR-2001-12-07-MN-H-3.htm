    JIM RAMSTAD, Republican, of Minnetonka, MN; born in Jamestown, ND, 
May 6, 1946; education: University of Minnesota, B.A., Phi Beta Kappa, 
1968; George Washington University, J.D. with honors, 1973; military 
service: first lieutenant, U.S. Army Reserves, 1968-74; elected to the 
Minnesota Senate, 1980; reelected 1982, 1986; assistant minority leader; 
attorney; adjunct professor; member: Ways and Means Committee; elected 
to the 102nd Congress, November 6, 1990; reelected to each succeeding 
Congress.
