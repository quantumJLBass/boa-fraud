    JOHN HICKENLOOPER, Democrat, of Denver, CO; born in Narberth, PA, 
February 7, 1952; education: B.A., english, Wesleyan University, 
Middletown, CT, 1974; M.A., geology, Wesleyan University, Middletown, 
CT, 1980; professional: geologist; entrepreneur; Mayor, Denver, CO, 
2003-11; Governor, State of Colorado, Denver, CO, 2011-19; married: 
Robin Pringle; children: Teddy; committees: Commerce, Science, and 
Transportation; Energy and Natural Resources; Health, Education, Labor, 
and Pensions; Small Business and Entrepreneurship; elected to the U.S. 
Senate on November 3, 2020.
