    BURGESS OWENS, Republican, of Salt Lake County, UT; born in 
Columbus, OH, August 2, 1951; education: B.S., University of Miami, 
Coral Gables, FL; professional: professional athlete; business 
executive; nonprofit executive; entrepreneur; religion: Church of Jesus 
Christ of Latter-Day Saints; children: Summur, Randii, Blair, Blake, 
Brett, and Brie; 15 grandchildren; caucuses: Direct Selling Caucus; 
Human Trafficking Caucus; Republican Study Committee; Suburban Caucus; 
Values Action Team; Western Caucus; committees: Education and Labor; 
Judiciary; elected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
