    ADRIAN SMITH, Republican, of Gering, NE; born in Scottsbluff, NE, 
December 19, 1970; education: graduated from Gering High School, Gering, 
1989; B.S., University of Nebraska, 1993; professional: business owner; 
education coordinator; Gering, NE, City Council, 1994-98; member of the 
Nebraska State Legislature, 1999-2007; married: Andrea McDaniel Smith; 
committees: Ways and Means; elected to the 110th Congress on November 7, 
2006; reelected to each succeeding Congress.
