    PETER A. DeFAZIO, Democrat, of Springfield, OR; born in Needham, 
MA, May 27, 1947; education: B.A., Tufts University, Middlesex County, 
MA, 1969; M.S., University of Oregon, Eugene, OR, 1977; professional: 
aide to Representative Jim Weaver, 1977-82; commissioner, Lane County, 
1983-86; married: Myrnie Daut; committees: chair, Transportation and 
Infrastructure; elected to the 100th Congress on November 4, 1986; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
