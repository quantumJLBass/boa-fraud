    SUSAN A. DAVIS, Democrat, of San Diego, CA; born on April 13, 1944, 
in Cambridge, MA; education: B.S., University of California at Berkeley; 
M.A., University of North Carolina; public service: served three terms 
in the California State Assembly; served nine years on the San Diego 
City School Board; former President of the League of Women Voters of San 
Diego; awards: California School Boards Association Legislator of the 
Year; League of Middle Schools Legislator of the Year; family: married 
to Steve; children: Jeffrey and Benjamin; grandson: Henry; elected to 
the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
