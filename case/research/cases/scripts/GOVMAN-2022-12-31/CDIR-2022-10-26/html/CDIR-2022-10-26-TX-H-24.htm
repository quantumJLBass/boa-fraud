    BETH VAN DUYNE, Republican, of Irving, TX; born in New York, NY, 
November 16, 1970; education: B.A., Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 
1995; professional: consultant; business executive; member of the 
Irving, TX, City Council, 2004-10; Mayor of Irving, TX, 2011-17; 
Regional Administrator, U.S. Department of Housing and Urban 
Development, 2017-19; family: two children; committees: Small Business; 
Transportation and Infrastructure; elected to the 117th Congress on 
November 3, 2020.
