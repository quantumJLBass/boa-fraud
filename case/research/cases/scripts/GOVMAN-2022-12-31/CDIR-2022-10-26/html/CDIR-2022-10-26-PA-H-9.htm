    DAN MEUSER, Republican, of Dallas, PA; born in Flushing, New York, 
NY, February 10, 1964; education: attended Maritime College, New York, 
NY, majored in maritime transportation / business, 1982-83; B.A., 
economics / government studies, on U.S. Navy ROTC scholarship, Cornell 
University, 1986; professional: 20 years of experience at Pride Mobility 
Products, Exeter, PA, Regional Manager, 1988-89, National Sales Manager, 
1989-92, Vice President of Sales, 1992-95, Senior Vice President, Sales 
and Marketing, 1995-2001, and President, 2001-08; Pennsylvania State 
Secretary, Revenue Department, Harrisburg, PA, 2011-15; committees: 
Foreign Affairs; Small Business; elected to the 116th Congress on 
November 6, 2018; reelected to the 117th Congress on November 3, 2020.
