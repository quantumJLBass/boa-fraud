    JENNIFFER GONZALEZ-COLON, Republican, of San Juan, PR; born in San 
Juan, August 5, 1976; education: B.A., political science, University of 
Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras, PR, 2001; J.D., Inter-American University of 
Puerto Rico, San Juan, PR, 2010; LL.M., Inter-American University of 
Puerto Rico, San Juan, PR, 2014; professional: member of the Puerto Rico 
House of Representatives, 2002-16; Speaker of the Puerto Rico House of 
Representatives, 2009-12; Minority Leader of the Puerto Rico House of 
Representatives, 2013-16; vice president of the PNP of Puerto Rico, 
2008-present; chair of the Puerto Rico Republican Party, 2015-21; 
religion: Catholic; committees: Natural Resources; Transportation and 
Infrastructure; elected to serve a four-year term in the 115th and 116th 
Congresses on November 8, 2016; reelected to serve a four-year term in 
the 117th and 118th Congresses on November 3, 2020.
