    TIMOTHY V. JOHNSON, Republican, of Sidney, IL; born on July 23, 
1946, in Champaign, IL; education: B.A., University of Illinois, Phi 
Beta Kappa; J.D., University of Illinois College of Law, graduated with 
high honors; occupation: Attorney; public service: Urbana, IL, City 
Council, 1971-75; Illinois House of Representatives, 1976-2000; Deputy 
Majority Leader; Champaign County, IL, Republican Party Chairman, 1990-
96; elected to the 107th Congress on November 7, 2000.
