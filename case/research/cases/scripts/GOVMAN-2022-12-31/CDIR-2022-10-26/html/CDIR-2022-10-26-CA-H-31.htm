    PETE AGUILAR, Democrat, of Redlands, CA; born in Fontana, San 
Bernardino County, CA, June 19, 1979; education: B.S., University of 
Redlands, Redlands, CA, 2001; professional: business owner; interim 
director and deputy director, Inland Empire Regional Office of the 
Governor of California, 2001; member of the Redlands, California City 
Council, 2006-14; Mayor of Redlands, CA, 2010-14; married: Alisha; 
children: Evan and Palmer; committees: Appropriations, House 
Administration; elected to the 114th Congress on November 4, 2014; 
reelected to each succeeding Congress.
